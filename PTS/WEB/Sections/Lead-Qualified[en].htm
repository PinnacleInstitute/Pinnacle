How to Generating Very Highly Qualified Leads!

Leads that come through a 5 step lead program are the most pre-qualified lead that you could possibly generate for your specific offer.  They are fresh, targeted, and most importantly they are educated on your offer.

Take your business or product and break it down into 5 easy steps for a prospect to understand.  Your prospects will go through each step with a simple click of their mouse.  No intense reading and the design won't bore your visitor.

Step 1(first page):  Welcome To "your company name"

This page usually consists of a powerful introduction to a few key benefits related to your offer.  This is the most important page of the presentation as it determines if a visitor will stay or go.  We can get them to stay.  After your prospect makes the decision to move further they can click the "Proceed To Step 2" Button.

Step 2:  What is "your company name"?

When your visitor clicks through to the second step they will be greeted with a very simple explanation on what your company is.  When they started, and just a general feel for who your company really is.  This page could also include some mention of your products or one particular product if you like.  This will lead the potential prospect directly to step 3.

Step 3:  Why "your product name"?

This is the portion of the 5 step lead program where you explain to the prospect WHY your product is of value to them or anyone else for that matter.  This is the page where you let them know the important and unique facts about your product and what it can or cannot do for them.

This is another very important part of the process as we are now directing the prospect into the opportunity portion of the 5 step program.

They will be directed smoothly into the 4th step. 

Step 4:  Why "your company name" as a business?

With this very important 4th step we convey to your potential prospect WHY your company and products would be a good choice for a home based business opportunity.  Immediate benefits to them are stressed and you must write copy that will empower your visitor to want to take action.

Step 5:  How Do I Get Started!

On this page it's really quite simple... It's at this point in time that we ask the visitor to take action and become a hot prospect for your business by having them fill out a detailed lead capture form.

Once the prospect submits their information as usual it will be delivered to you in real-time as a fresh lead.  Waiting to be called by you.

How's That For Very Highly Qualified Prospects?

By engaging your visitors and walking them through the simple 5 step process you can "pre-educate" prospects on your business and products.

Just imagine speaking with prospects that knew...

  • Who You Are.
  • What Your Company Is.
  • What Your Company Sells.
  • Why Your Products Are Great.
  • And They Want To Join!

BEFORE they submitted their information on your lead program.

You will never find a lead like that available for purchase through a lead retailer.  There is only one way to get leads of this quality and that is to generate them yourself.

View Sales Team

Overview

You can view your Sales Team Organization(s) by selecting the "Display My" list option for the selected team.  You can view up to four levels of hierarchy within your sales team at a time.  Select the "1 Level" to "4 Levels" options to display the selected number or levels in your organization.

View Beyond Your First 4 Levels

You can click the name of any display team member to view 1 - 4 levels of team members below them.

Search Team Members

You can search for a team member anywhere down in your organization.  Select the option to search by member name or member #.  For the member name option enter characters up to the first name followed by a space, and the last name.  You can enter a partial entry and the system will search for the first member matching your specified name.  For example; If you are searching for Joe blackburn you can enter "joe blackburn", "joe b",  "joe", etc.

To search by member #, enter the desired member's unique member number and click the "Search" button.

The system will search for the specified member anywhere in your sales organization.  If the member is not found, or is found but is not in your organization, you will receive an error message.

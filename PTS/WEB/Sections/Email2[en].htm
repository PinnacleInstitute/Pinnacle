Text Email Address

The system uses the member's text email address to send all mentoring and prospecting notices.  Mentoring notices include when a new mentor is selected by the member and when the member has an accomplishment that the mentor has requested to be notified about.  These include signing into the system, completing a class, completing an assessment, completing a certification, completing a goal, closing a prospect, etc.

The text email address is the same as the primary email address by default.  The text email address can be changed to a cell phone email address to receive the mentoring and prospecting notices as a text message on a cell phone.  The format for the cell phone email address varies per provider as outlined below.

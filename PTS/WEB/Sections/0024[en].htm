Network Marketing - Formula for Success


Overview

Hundreds of new network marketing companies start up every year and the vast majority fails within the first 12 – 18 months. Why do so many fail and more importantly how do the successful companies stay around for many years. The number one reason for these failures is mismanagement. Most network marketing companies are started by individuals that have experienced some success as a distributor. The transformation from distributor to corporate executive is usually disastrous.

The following three fundamental cornerstones of Management, Product, and Compensation Plan must be in place for a network marketing company to flourish for the long-term. An individual seeking an income opportunity in network marketing should carefully analyze these to insure that their emotional, financial and time investment is not wasted.

Management

1. Trustworthy – The management team of the Company must have integrity and be willing to always tell the truth regardless off how difficult the situation.

2. Competent – The management team must be experienced and capable of managing the tactical and strategic business objectives.

Product

1. High Demand – The product should have mass-market appeal to provide a broad potential customer base.

2. Consumable – The product should be regularly consumed to generate residual revenue for the sales force.

3. Quality – The product must be of high quality and content to generate excitement and passion for the product by the customers and distributors.

4. Value – The product must have retail value of at least what it is being sold for and is attractive to outside individuals for retail sale.

Compensation Plan

1. Legal – The compensation plan must emphasis product sales and cannot promote money games.

2. Attract Workers – The compensation plan must attract distributors that are willing to work hard for a personally rewarding experience. It must not be based on “free money” or “no work required” promises that only attract non-workers and get-rich-quick junkies.

3. Profitable – The compensation plan must be profitable quickly for the distributors to cover their costs.

4. Stability – The compensation plan must provide stability with the ability to earn $300 - $500 monthly for the core of the distributors.

5. Excitement – The compensation plan must provide excitement with the ability to earn $50,000+ monthly for the motivated sales leaders and dream builders.

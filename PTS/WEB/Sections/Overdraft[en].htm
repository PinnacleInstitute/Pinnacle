Overdraft Protection

Select the Overdraft Protection feature if you would like the following financial protection.

1. Don't Miss Bonuses:  Overdraft Protection will ensure that your personal sales volume will never drop below $40 in the event that one of your retail customers cancels and causes your personal sales volume to drop below $40.  In the event that this happens, Overdraft Protection will automatically adjust your personal purchase to insure a minimum of $40 in personal sales volume.

2. Don't Bill Your Credit Card: Overdraft Protection will automatically take your monthly billing from your earnings and will not bill your credit card unless the earnings are not sufficient to cover the balance due.

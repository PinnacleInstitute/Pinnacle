Name Registration Tutorial
 Ready to get started?  Okay, the first step is to choose a suitable domain name that you can share with others, something that they will easily remember [i.e.  Any-Domain-Name .com].  Have a few in mind, in case your first choice is already taken.
   First Choice __________________ .COM
   Second Choice _______________ .COM
   Third Choice _________________ .COM
You may want to consider a name with a different extension besides .COM, such as .NET  .ORG  .BIZ  .INFO
Open up GoDaddy.com in a new browser by clicking here.

Start domain name search by entering your first choice and pressing "Go!"  (see example to the right)
[If your browser blocks Pop-Ups, you may need to hold down the Control (Ctrl) key as you Click, to allow the window to appear.]

The next page will indicate whether the Domain name is available.  If it is available, you must place a checkmark in the box under your desired extension (.com is the most common choice)  Please note the price per extension per year.  [See the examples below]
You will have the option of choosing additional variations of the same Domain name.  (You should be aware that almost every step will have an option to upgrade or upsell your registration.)

If the domain name IS available, you will see a message similar to this:  If the domain name is NOT available, you will see a message similar to this: 

You may then have the option of purchasing related domain names at a discount by click on "Yes! Add these to my order."  If you choose to only register for your originally selected Domain name, click the text "No thanks. Continue to checkout..."
[click here to see example]

 On the next page, "Domain Registration Information", you will be asked to enter your name and contact information.  If you already have a GoDaddy account, you can logon at this point. If not, enter your name, email address, and other contact information.  When finished, press Continue. You can confirm or edit your contact info on the following page.


NOTE : Your session may expire following 20 minutes of inactivity at any time during the checkout process, and you will have to restart the registration.

 The "Registration and Checkout Options" page allows you to set the Registration Length for each purchased domain name.  You can also choose whether the account will Auto Renew and the end of each year, or whether you'll renew manually. You may also choose a 'Standard Registration' or a 'Private Registration'. A 'Standard Registration' simply means that your name, phone number, postal and email address are available to anyone looking in the public WHOIS directory. For a yearly fee, such information may be kept hidden from public view by choosing 'Private Registration'.

After choosing type of registration, you may select your Checkout Preference, either "Show me exclusive offers that will help me get the most from my domain" or "Quick Checkout".  The exclusive offers include such items as Web Hosting services, Email Accounts, WebSite building utilities, marketing, site security, and an online store. For our purpose here, we have selected the Quick Checkout option. [Click here to see an example of Quick Checkout]

You may then be given another opportunity to upgrade from a Standard registration to a Private registration, if you didn't do so already.  [Click here to see an example of the Private Registration page]

 Upon choosing "Add to Cart", or "No thanks. Continue to checkout...", you will arrive at the Secure Checkout. You will:
1) Review your shopping cart, 2) Select your Payment Method, and 3) affirm that you have read the Legal Agreements, such as "Domain Registration Agreement" and/or the "Universal Terms of Service".  [A promo code for up to 10% Off is: SB2006 ]  You may proceed either by clicking "Checkout Now".  

Next, enter your LoginID, password, Password hint and email [Click here to see an example of Login/Password fields]

Then provide your Credit card information (Name, card number, card type, and expiration date)  [Click here to see an example of Billing Information]  To continue with the order, as part of the security process, you may be asked to enter the provided Access Code.

 The next page displays Your Order Confirmation, with Customer Number, Order Number, Login Name, and your Password Hint, (partially masked).  You may want to Print (Ctrl+P) this page, for your own personal record.
 
If you have addtional questions about your registration, you may request a phone call from the GoDaddy new customer orientation staff by filling out the form on the right.  Otherwise click on "Login to my account".  When you next logon, you may be asked for a four digit PIN for security purposes [see below]

This concludes the registration process for a GoDaddy account and a Domain Name.

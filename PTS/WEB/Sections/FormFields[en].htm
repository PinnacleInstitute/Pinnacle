Form Fields

Any Information Page may be defined as a form with custom defined fields.  The form may be for members or for prospects and may be available to all members or to company administrators only.

Custom Fields

A list of custom fields may be defined for the form to be used for data entry or for auto-fill-in of member and/or prospect information.  The following member and prospect data fields are available.  Extra fields are also available as defined by the Company's custom fields for members and by the Prospect Type's custom fields for prospects.

Field Names

Each custom field has an assigned name to be referenced in the form.  Field names can be entered in the form enclosed is curly brackets {} to be relaced with the field data referenced by the field name.

Examples

FirstName=Member.NameFirst 
LastName=Member.NameLast 
DOB=Member.Extra.DOB
ProspectName=Prospect.ProspectName

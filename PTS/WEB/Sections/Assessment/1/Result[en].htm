Your Assessment Result is calculated in four different traits.

C = Cautious    
A = Aggressive 
P = Persuasive 
S = Systematic 

The highest of the four scores is the behavior which might best describe you. The closer the 4 scores are to each other, the more traits from each profile will apply to you. The farther apart the scores are the more prominent the traits are for the higher score.

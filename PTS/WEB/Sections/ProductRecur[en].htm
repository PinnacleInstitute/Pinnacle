Recurring Product Options

Overview
A product may be specified to collect multiple payments over time.  Recurring payments may start immediately and continue forever or they may start after a specific time period and/or continue for a specific period of time. The options to define the term for the recurring payments is define in the "Term" text box.

Recurring Payment Time Period
The "Recur" list box specifies if the payment is recurring (leave blank for non-recurring payments) or select weekly or monthly recurring payments.

Indefinite Recurring Payments
Leave the "Term" text box empty to start the recurring payments immediately and continue forever.

Delayed Recurring Payments
Specify the number of days, weeks or months to wait before the first payment is processed. for example; 7d, 2w or 1m specify to wait 7 days, 2 weeks or 1 month.

Limited Recurring Payments
Specify the number of payments is be processed following a dash. for example; -3 specifies to start immediately and process three payments. 1m-2 specifies to start in 1 month and process 2 payments.

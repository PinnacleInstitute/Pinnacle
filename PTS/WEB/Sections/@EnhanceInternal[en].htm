Pinnacle Internal Enhancements!

(updated 8/1/06 8:00am CST)

8/1/05   Free Membership Features: Each company can have specified which features are available for its free memberships.
8/1/05   Multiple Membership Levels: A company can have upto 3 levels of membership.  The retail price, retail priofit, and available features can be specified for each level.  The member has a level specified.  Level 1 is the main level.  Free memberships have a level of 0.
4/6/05   Pinnacle Sales System Member Commissions and Payouts: The member welcome page supports links to view their commissions and payouts. Only applicable to members of the Pinnacle Sales System.
4/6/05  Member Commissions & Payouts: The member detail page supports links to view their commissions and payouts. Only applicable to members of the Pinnacle Sales System.
4/6/05  New Company from Prospect: You can create a new company from an existing prospect.
4/6/05  New Affiliate from Prospect: You can create a new affiliate from an existing prospect.
4/6/05  Company Affiliate -> Member: Company now have a member rather than an affiliate. 
4/5/05  Removed Affiliates: The Pinnacle sales people are no longer affiliates, they are just members of the Pinnacle Sales Solution. 
12/29/05  Member Search by Phone 1: users can search for members by their phone 1. 
10/20/05 Member Support Search Engine: Companies may now be given the option to provide the Member Support Search Engine with the Free Development Membership.
10/20/05 Document Search by Date: Documents can be searched by date for company, trainer or course documents.
10/19/05 Assessment Recommended Courses: The System will now disregard duplicate recommended course ids that may be specified in the assessment, question or choice.
8/4/05 Company Commission Rate: Can now enter a specific commision rate to pay the salesperson.
8/2/05 Company Subnet: Each Company can specify a subnet on the Company profile page for accessing their Pinnacle training website.
8/2/05 Payments- Billing Date: You can now specify the paid thru date to create payments on the Accounting Reports page. If it is not specified, it defaults to today.
8/1/05 Commissions - Mgmt Bonuses: There are no longer any mgmt bonuses calculated.
7/18/05 New Assessment Question Choices: When adding a new Asessment question with choices, you can now specify the point value of each choice.
6/16/05 Class Complete Calculation: The class will ignore lessons of 0 length to determine if the entire class is complete. Lessons that are merely quick/setup instructions should have a length of 0.  Lesson page has help text that lesson length of 0 = NO lesson content.
6/16/05 Class Lesson automated complete checkbox: If the lessom length is > 6 the complete checkbosx will be automatically checked at 3 minutes, if length <= 1 checked at 30 seconds, if length is between 1 and 6 minutes checked at half the lesson length.    
6/16/05 Member Search by Business/Group/Role: Can search for members by business membership #, Group or Role.
6/14/05 Company Sales Commission Type: You can specify the type of sales commission to pay on the customer. (25%, 25%-10%, 10%, Manual)
6/5/05 Company Business Account Defaults: You can specify company default values for a new business membership including;  if business memberships are available, wholesale price, retail price, number of included accounts, additional account wholesale price, additional account retail price.  
5/4/05 Prospects: Manage prospects for affiliates. 
5/2/05 Activity Reminders: Create a reminder note for work to be done for a member, company, affiliate, or trainer. 
5/2/05 Activity Reports: View list of activities for an employee for a specified date range. 
4/11/05 Commission Links: Commission list for an affiliate will link to detail page if employee clicks on commission date.  
4/11/05 Locked/Frozen Notes: Added support for notes locked for manager editing only and freezing to the top of the list.
4/9/05  Notes: Created unlimited notes for Members and Affiliates

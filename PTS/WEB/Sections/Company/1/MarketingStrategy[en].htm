Marketing Strategy

What exactly is our product and what does it do?



What is our target market and what is its personality?



What benefit is our target market looking to get out of our product?



What is our Unique Selling Proposition – What makes our product different, better, or more desirable than other similar products available?



How will this be conveyed to the customer?



How will we prove it?



How will it stand out above its competitors?



Who are the top competitors and what are they doing right and what are they doing wrong?

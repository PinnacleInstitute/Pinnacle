You can have a personal mentor to assist you in meeting your personal and professional objectives.  Your Mentor can help you:
•   assess your strengths & weaknesses,
•   identify your needs and wants,
•   create an action plan, and
•   set and track your goals.

You and your Mentor can also post notes on your mentoring progress and plans.

You can also be a mentor to others.  Note: While any member may be mentored, only Performance members may be Mentors.

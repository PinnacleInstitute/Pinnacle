Sell the Products and Opportunity 

Unlike marketing, which focuses on identifying and attracting potential customers and partners, Sales is the process of acquiring and converting a prospect into a customer or partner.

Sales Sequence
After your marketing efforts have identified the target market and defined how to get in front of them, how to get their attention, and how to get their interest, you will be ready to transform suspects into prospects and to proceed into the sales process of converting prospects into customers.

The sales process consists of the following sequence of steps.

  • Share the Benefits
  • Qualify the Prospect
  • Present the Solution
  • Experience the Product
  • Close the Sale

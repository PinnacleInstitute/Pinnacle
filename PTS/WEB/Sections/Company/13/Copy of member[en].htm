The EcoQuest Training Network (ETN) is a learning environment similar to college. You first look through the course catalog, select the courses you wish to take and register for those courses. 

To begin, click on My Classes. Then click on the link,  Click Here to view the Course Catalog at the top middle of the page. Or simply use the menu bar on the left. Expand each category folder you wish to explore; then find the courses you wish to take and click register. Your registered course list will appear in My Classes.

Please enjoy the large library of courses and don't forget about the Suggestion Boxes, Online Conference Rooms and Discussion Boards.

If you have questions along the way, click the Frequently Ask Questions link above or on menu on the left of the screen.

Finally please click on My Profile above, and review your information and make any corrections.

If you have any questions or need assistance please contact us at support@pinnaclep.com.

Proven Formula for Success  
BUSINESS SEQUENCE

The sequence of steps that you execute to build your business is critical to your success.  Many businesses fail because they don’t understand the principles of business sequence.  A successful sale is like building a house; the paint is applied to the finished woodwork, which is built on the sheetrock and framing, which is supported by a solid foundation.  The paint is never applied to the framing nor is the wallpaper applied to the foundation.  Not only is the correct sequence of the steps important, but also the correct tools for each step are essential.  Prospecting with the wrong DVD, literature or sales pitch is like painting a house with a hammer. 

The ESN Proven Formula for Success is designed to provide a sequence of steps in just the right order, for the right objectives and with the right supporting tools.  This will insure success rates similar to those experienced by typical franchises for anyone willing to work hard and make the necessary time and money investments.

The basic steps in the direct sales business sequence are:

  • Support
    		•

    Establish an effective support system 
  • Prepare
    		•  Get ready for your new home-based business 
  • Prospect
    		•  Attract qualified prospects 
  • Sell
    		•  Convert prospects into customers 
  • Service
    		•  Keep satisfied customers with quality service 
  • Improve
    		•  Improve your business constantly


    Support

    The “Get Started Now” phase of your Formula for Success establishes an effective support system for you and your business.  You will identify a personal mentor to help you along the way.  You will identify your business objective and your available resources in time, money and skills.  You will define a set of goals to guide you in the process.  Establishing a solid support foundation will give you a resource to fallback on when the inevitable problems arise.

    Prepare

    The “Prepare for Success” phase of your Formula for Success gets you ready to succeed in your business.  You will accomplish a checklist of setup activities for your business.  You will take the necessary basic training that every new business owner must receive to be successful.  You will define a specific action plan with your mentor that meets your specific business objectives.  Getting ready for your business will have you prepared and organized to build your successful business.

    Prospect

    The “Market Your Products” phase of your Formula for Success will help you identify exactly who needs your products and opportunity, where they are, how to get their attention in a few seconds, and how to get their interest in a few minutes.  After you get their interest you can start selling.  You will have lead capture programs to help you determine if your prospect is really interested. 

    Sell

    The “Sell Your Products” phase of your Formula for Success will have you converting qualified prospects into customers and partners.  You will be prepared to present the solution to them, help them experience the products, and close the sale.  You and your mentor will be able to track all your prospects through each step of the sales process and report on your progress.

    Service

    The “Provide Quality Service” phase of your Formula for Success establishes your business longevity by insuring that your customers and partners are completely satisfied with the products or opportunity, and that they are receiving maximum benefit from them.  Satisfied customers will continue to buy from you and will provide precious referrals to build your business.

    Improve

    The “Improve Your Plan” phase of your Formula for Success gives you the competitive edge by constantly evaluating what is working well for you and what can be improved.  You will have continual access to a nearly unlimited library of professional and personal assessments, certifications, and interactive multimedia training courses.

     

    Why Business Fail

    Businesses fail because they do not understand and properly execute their business sequence.  If any step of the sequence is performed out of order, or for the wrong objective, or with the wrong tools, the sale is lost and the business fails.  The following real examples exhibit some of these failures.

    1. No one to talk to about prospect objections.
    2. Not knowing how to correctly complete paperwork. 
    3. Wasting advertising dollars on people who don’t need the solution.
    4. Not knowing the prospect’s needs. 
    5. Presenting the wrong solution. 
    6. Making presentations to unqualified prospects. 
    7. Talking too much. 
    8. Trying to close the sale to early. 
    9. Doing a feature-dump during product demonstrations. 
    10. Prospecting with a DVD designed for a different purpose. 
    11. Not able to get the prospect’s attention in a few seconds. 
    12. Not able to concisely describe why they are unique. 
    13. Not drawing the prospect into the value proposition to get their interest.


    The ESN Proven Formula for Success will help you execute the sequence of steps in just the right order, for the right objectives and with the right supporting tools.

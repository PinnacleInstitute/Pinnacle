Good Morning Jerry,

Thank you so much for your order!  Your FreshAir air purification unit should arrive via UPS this morning.  Since I am not there in person, I would like to introduce you to some of the features and give you some instruction to setting it up.

  1. The machine has 2 buttons for setting Ozone that contain 3 distinct settings.  The first button (bottom left hand side) has “normal” and “high”.  The second button is on the bottom right hand side, and says “away mode”.  Ozone is the basic substance that will kill bacteria and germs in the air and neutralize odors.
  2. If the display indicates “NORMAL” it is in the lowest setting and little Ozone is being emitted.
  3. If the Normal/High button is pushed again, the word “NORMAL” disappears and a simple graph is displayed.  This is the high mode.
  4. The purifier up/down buttons indicated the desired square footage the machine should be purifying.

 

 

  1. If odors are strong, it will take higher levels of Ozone to neutralize them and to purify the room.
  2. Thus when people and pets are absent from the premise, the “Away” mode should be utilized, initially for an 8 hour period.  To accomplish this,  push the “Away” mode four times, and ascertain that the display indicates 8.0.
  3. At the end of eight hours, the unit will revert back to the setting it was on before the “Away” mode was initialized, i.e. either “Normal” or “High”
  4. If the smell is a strong chlorine-like odor, the area  has been well sanitized and the unit may be turned down to more acceptable levels.  This is accomplished by (a) going from “high” to “normal” by pushing the left button until “Normal” appears in the display, or (b) reducing the “purifier” button to a lower square foot level, or (c) use the away mode for times less than 8 hours 2,4, and 6 are available, or (d) a combination of the above.
  5. No two situations will be exactly alike, and no exact formula can be provided.  The range of purification would go from a low setting of “Normal”/250 to “Away”/3000, with “High”/1500 being somewhere in the middle. It is up to the user to find their own comfort zone.

Initially, insert the batteries into the remote.  Insert the power cord firmly in to the back of the unit.  Place the unit on the highest location in the room making sure it is level and has enough room behind for proper ventilation.  I usually anglel the unit caddy corner so it is aimed at the largest part of the area to be covered.  For today, I would set the bottom left button to "normal" and the top right button to a low to medium fan setting.  This gives you a chance to become familiar with the unit and the delicious sweet "thunderstorm fresh" scent produced by the ionization process.  To address the odor you mentioned previously, tonight before you leave,(providing the room will remain unoccupied), press the away mode button until you reach 8 hours (or less if you work late), set the purification button to 3000 s/f and set the fan at the highest setting.  Tomorrow you should notice a drastic change in the room.  If the odor is not completely gone, you can repeat the process that night.  If your room is small, I would leave the setting on normal and if you want to set it on high, set it for no more than 250 s/f or whatever you feel the square footage is.

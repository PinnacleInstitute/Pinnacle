How do I find
Qualified Prospects?

 

Attract Qualified Prospects like a Magnet!

Identify prospects that have already exhibited a predisposition to your value proposition.  Focus on prospects that have already purchased related products or services.  You wouldn’t waste your time selling cigars to non-smokers or steak to vegetarians, would you?  Exchange valuable information with your potential prospects for their contact info in order to start a relationship.

But, after you attract them what do you do with them?


Build your Qualified Warm Market!

Establish an ongoing relationship in order to build trust and position yourself as an expert by continually providing useful information that they want.  19 out of 20 people you meet for the first time are not ready to buy from you.  Is 95% of your revenue walking out the door?  Or are you capturing the relationship?

Your prospects will pay you to market to them…


Self-fund your Marketing Activities!

Make your marketing activities pay for themselves.  As a positioned expert you can offer helpful advice, tips & techniques, and recommendations.  These recommendations may include useful information, products or services that your affiliate partners provide.  The income from your affiliate fees will fund your marketing promotions, voila – “Perpetual Marketing”.  

Now your prospect is ready …


Capitalize at the Right Time!

Offer your value proposition only after you have established a trusted relationship and only after your qualified prospect has identified a problem that you can solve.  You will be in a far better position as a trusted expert to suggest possible solutions that may lead to your ultimate sale.  Capitalizing is the easy part after all the previous steps have been executed correctly.  Unfortunately, this is where most businesses fail by attempting to capitalize at the first contact.

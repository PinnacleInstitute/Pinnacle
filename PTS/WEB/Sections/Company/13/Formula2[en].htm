  Action Plan
After you've completed your basic training you will be ready to create your action plan.   Select your "Getting Started" goal and click on the "Action Plan" sub goal from your Goal Management System.  This will provide a list of additional sub goals for each action plan item.  Click the "[edit]" link for each item to enter your response in the description and the completion date as you complete each setup item.  Add an optional note for each action plan item to describe additional details.

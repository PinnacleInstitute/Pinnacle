How do I Register for a Class?  

-    Click on My Classes
-    Click on “Click Here to View Course Catalog”
-    Click on folders to expand and view the contents of that folder
-    Click on company’s folder to view Private Courses, required courses, and recommended courses by the company.
-    Classes will be listed below the catalogue, to view details on a class, click on the class’ name.
-    To register for the class, click on register to the right of the class name.
-    Click return, and then either continue searching for classes, or click “Return to My Classes” at the top.

How do I Take a Class? 

-    Click on My Classes
-    Click on the class that you have already registered for that you wish to take.
-    Click on the lesson that you are on to begin the course.
-    From the lesson page you are able to navigate through lessons using the “Next Lesson” button, or the “Go to Lesson”  scroll bar.
-    You can also use the check box at the bottom to choose whether to mark the lesson complete or not.
-    If the lesson does not load, be sure to completely read the instructions, there may be something special required to view it.
-    When you have completed all the lessons in the course a screen will prompt you to rate the course.
-    After returning to the lessons page you will be able to print a certificate for the course by clicking on “Print Certificate.”
-    From here you are able to type the name that you wish to appear on the certificate, and then click “Print Certificate.”
-    From the lessons page you are also able to change your evaluation of the course by clicking on “View Evaluation,” and you can remove the course from you list with the “Remove” button.

How do I Use a Conference Room? 

-    Click on Conference Rooms.
-    Select the room you wish to enter.
-    It may take a while, but wait for the room to load, or you may need to download the latest version of Java using the link at the bottom.
-    You can edit your user profile with the button in the top right, and you can also shoot to a discussion board with the button next to it.
-    Use the text box at the bottom to type your messages, and enter or the send button.
-    Some tools to change the font and other things are just above the text box.
-    To send a private message to someone, click on their name, and then click private message.
-    A separate chat box will appear, and you can use that to send private messages

How do I Create a discussion board Thread?  

-    Click on Discussion Boards.
-    Choose the discussion board you wish to create a new thread in.
-    Click add new discussion in the top right corner.
-    In the top text box put the subject, and in the one beneath, put the message.  Then click add.
-    You can also edit your profile and jump to the conference rooms in the top right corner.
-    To reply to a message click on the discussion, and then click reply to the message you wish to reply to.
-    From this same page you can also edit messages previously written by you.
-    By clicking on your own name you can create a signature or edit one that has been made.

How do I Take an Assessment?  

-    Click on My Assessments.
-    Click Take New Assessment in the top right corner.
-    Click on the assessment you wish to take.
-    Follow the instructions through each question and click continue to progress through the assessment.
-    When you are done with the assessment, you can view your results and then click return to my assessments.
-    From here, depending on the limits set for the assessment, you can retake any assessments that were previously taken, or take a new one.

How do I print a certificate?   

After you complete all the lessons for your class you will have the option to print a certificate from the class page. Click the "Print Certificate" button to confirm your name as you would like it to appear on the certificate. A "Print Dialog Box" is displayed for you to select the desired printer. If you pass the optional quiz(es), you will be able to print a "Certificate of Achievement", otherwise you will be able to print a "Certificate of Completion".

How do I view the Certificate before I print it?   

Pinnacle automatically starts the printing process after you confirm your name as it should appear on the certificate, by displaying the "Print Dialog Box". Cancel this Dialog Box and select the "File / Print Preview" menu option to view the certificate before printing.

What do I do if I get a downloading error when printing a certificate?   

Pinnacle dowloads an Active X control to automatically set the printing formatting options for the certificate. If you have a problem downloading this file, you can manually set the printing options. On the "Printing Certificate..." page, cancel the "Print Dialog Box" and select the "File / Page Setup" menu option. Remove the header and footer information, select the "Landscape" orientation, and set all margins to 1/2 inch. Select the "File / Print" or "File / Print Preview" menu option to print the certificate.

How do I print a framable certificate?   

Pinnacle certificates are printed with a 1/2 inch margin on a 8 1/2 x 11 inch paper. This will frame nicely in a 8 1/2 x 11 frame or a 8 x 10 frame. If you would like the certificate to center exactly in a 8 x 10 frame, set the left and right margins to .75 inches by selecting the "File / Page Setup" menu option in your browser.

How do I print a good quality certificate?   

For best printing results, use a color printer and bright white card stock paper (80 - 110 lb.). You may also try printing on light colored paper like a nice parchment stock. These special papers can be purchased at any office supply store.

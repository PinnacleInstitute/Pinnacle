About Pinnacle Performance Institute

The Pinnacle Performance Institute shows companies how to clone their top performers to significantly increase their profits.  It does this with a very affordable online Performance Management Solution customized to their specific need in order to Measure the strengths and weaknesses, Build confidence and competence, and Communicate throughout the entire organization.  The Pinnacle Performance Solution empowers companies to Close the Gap between Vision and Execution by exposing reality... and acting upon it.

Pinnacle maintains an extensive library of training and educational courses from top trainers around the world on hundreds of topics related to self-improvement and personal & professional growth. Members of Pinnacle have unlimited access to the archived library of audio and video training courses for a small monthly tuition. The Pinnacle library of training courses provides a great value to its members that would normally cost them hundreds to thousands of dollars.

Training Courses
Pinnacle has developed a unique “Free Market” platform for providing and acquiring its breadth of training courses from top trainers around the world. The Pinnacle computer system tracks the popularity and member feedback of all training courses to provide a ranking for each course. Members benefit from the feedback of other members enabling them to focus on the best training courses for their needs. Members may also choose to complete certification tests and receive printable certificates.

The training providers benefit from the unique Free Market platform by being directly compensated by Pinnacle based on the value of their training courses. Ten percent (10%) of the total revenues from monthly tuitions is equitably distributed to the training providers based on their course evaluations and rankings. Training providers also benefit from the marketing of their additional training products and services to the members who have participated in their regular Pinnacle training courses.

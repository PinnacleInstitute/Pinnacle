Course Goals

Participants will learn how to use guided imagery, physical relaxation techniques, self-applied acupressure, and cognitive strategies to reduce and eliminate self-sabotaging emotional eating patterns.  

Course Materials

Participants will need audio capability on their computer for the audio exercises in some lessions.   A note book to keep a log and to record your observations.

Evaluation

At the end of the course there will be an opportunity to evaluate your learning and even to learn what aspects might benefit from a review.

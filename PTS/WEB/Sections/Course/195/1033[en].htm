Most of us, not all, but most, get into the field of healing because we have needed healing in our lives at one time or another.  This lesson is going to show some of the ways that an individual might look for healing ones self.

 

There is an array of modalities to choose from for self healing.  One of my favorites in traditional talk therapy.

Yes you can go to a trained therapist to get the help you need, but first I suggest that you practice voicing yourself to a trusted frind.  If you do not have any friends that you casn trust, then you are probably hanging out with the wrong people.  I have a rule in my life.  I don't spend time with people who are not going to add to my life in some way.

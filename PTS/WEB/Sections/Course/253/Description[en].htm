Course Goals

Using this audio, you can attend a meditation class with Shirley Knapp. She'll guide you through the steps to be in trust and unconditional love without fear. This will help you to live more intuitively on your soul path and in Joy.

The hour long class with the spoken word will add a new dimension to Shirley’s book Sustaining Joy.

During the session, Shirley's soulful, clear and gentle voice will assist you to go deep within yourself into the meditative state. There you can receive insights and release the fears or blockages that prevent you from listening to your intuition.

The class offers new exercises in addition to those in the book and will assist in taking the next steps on the path to no fear! It moves the abstract concept of coming to trust into the day to day realm of our own lives. You can use the meditations later on your own. You'll find that you'll expand on what you've been exposed to. As you begin to trust your intuition more, you'll "go with the flow" during your meditations. You'll find yourself listening to this audio over and over again, as each time you'll gain new insight.

Technical Requirements

You will need to be able to login and have a telephone line available for the sessions that are facilitated by the instructor.

Course Requirements

Complete a Life Misssion, Vision and Values Statement - 40%

Complete 3 Quizzes - 20%

Attendance in three webinars - 30%

View two courses online - 10%

Course Goals

Identify the stages of life transitions

Identify support structures to aid with transitions

To clarify what resources you have available to fulfil your purpose

To develop new attitude matix to interact with your core values

To develop a path for the student to take control of their life

To assist the student to establish emotional discipline and control

Course Materials

Course workbook to be provided by the instructor

Grading Policy

By completing the course requirements you will receive a grade based on your use of the concepts taught and the workbook that will be provided.

The Correct Questions To Ask:

  • Problem
  • Level(s) of anxiety/agitation
  • Gaps between present state and desired level
  • Specific needs that are being meet and are yet unfilled
  • What, when, how and under what conditions will they buy
  • Level of objection/opposition you will face
  • Competitive forces/factors
  • Level of correlation between pre-call data gathering and actual perception that prospect has of situation
  • Provide sales with the confidence to feel in control of the sales situation

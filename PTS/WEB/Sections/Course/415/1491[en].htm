Inadequate Pre-Call Planning:

  • Enter at wrong level
  • Display ignorance of account
  • Make erroneous assumptions and claims
  • Become timid and say nothing
  • Call on “unqualified prospects”
  • “Blind-sided” by past problems
  • Enter with unrealistic expectations
  • Enter at wrong end of buying cycles
  • Don’t understand prospects reasons for buying
  • Arrive with the wrong sales tools
  • Look foolish in front of prospects
  • Don’t convey professionalism or quality

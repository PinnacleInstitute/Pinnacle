A husband struggles to find a suitable place for his young wife to give birth...

Far away, wise men search the heavens for a sign that will lead them to the newborn king...

Lowly shepards wonder at a new star...

Then humbly, unnoticed by most, Jesus Christ, the Savior of the world, is born.

The story of the nativity is familiar to people everywhere, and is taken primarily from the second chapter of Luke as found in the King James version of The Holy Bible.  Luke II was filmed on location in the Holy Land, where the sacred events occurred.

Technical Requirements

Hardware: Intel Pentium 200 MHz processor or equivalent, 32 MB of RAM (memory), 16 bit sound card (to hear audio clips and voice overs).  Software: Windows 95, 98, 2000, XP, NT 4.0 with SP 3, 4 or 5; fourth generation browser with DHTML support.

Course Goals

Recognize how your company's privacy policy affects the way you handle customer data.  Identify the elements of a record retention policy.

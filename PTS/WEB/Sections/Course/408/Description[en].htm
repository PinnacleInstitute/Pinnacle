Course Requirements

Students must complete each assignment and demonstrate an understanding of the materials. At least one test will accompany each lesson.

Course Goals

The goal of this course is to make home users safer and more familiar with the internet and their own computers.

Course Materials

This course will include: A lesson, an assignment and at least one test. There will be recommended reading and links to sites, designed to increase your understanding of this course.

Grading Policy

 

Grading will be on a pass/fail basis. You must make at least 80% to pass successfully.

A certificate of completion will be issued upon completion of this class.

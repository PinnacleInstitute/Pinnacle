Results of Not Planning

Results of not planning are:

  1. Dependents and possessions inadequately protected against risk.

  2. Financial goals and objectives not reached since money is not routinely set aside for them or a lack of financial objectives which make long-term goals seem unattainable.

  3. Wasted resources since no plan means day-to-day decision making may not be done with an eye to the future.

  4. Higher income and estate taxes paid which might have been avoided with a financial plan.

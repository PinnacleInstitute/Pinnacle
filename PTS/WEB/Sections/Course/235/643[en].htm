To Alleviate Anxiety

  1. View anxiety as a normal reaction to stressful events and give yourself permission to feel anxious during difficult times.
  2. Realize that most decisions are neither "right" nor "wrong", but just lead to different experiences which have advantages and disadvantages.
  3. Think in terms of hoping for rather than expecting events to happen in a certain way.
  4. Plan for the future and do something about it instead of worrying about it.
  5. Plan for less than you can do in a particular day and make anything extra a bonus.
  6. Look for enjoyment in the "doing" of something rather than focusing so much on the end result.
  7. Speak, eat, drive and move at a more relaxed pace.
  8. Prepare for morning the night before.
  9. Arrive at your class, work, or appointment 10 minutes early.
  10. Go for a brisk walk or engage in some other physical activity involving large muscle movement when feeling anxious.
  11. Use abdominal breathing to calm anxious feelings.
  12. Pray for patience in dealing with anxious situations.
  13. Practice in your mind performing successfully before you enter a particular performance situation.
  14. View mistakes as opportunities for learning rather than personal failures.
  15. Cheer for rather than compete with others.
  16. Get involved with other people in worthwhile endeavors.
  17. Smile since smiling reduces anxiety.
  18. Use good thought control by giving yourself such statements as:
    1. Five years from now, who will care.
    2. I can do what I can do and that is all.
    3. What other ways can I view this situation?
    4. I'm not going to waste my energy worrying about it.
    5. Getting upset will not help me deal with the situation.
    6. Things are usually not as bad as I anticipate them to be.


  19. Obtain professional help, and possible medication, if your anxiety persists and does not seem related to any changing circumstances or stressful situations in your life.
  20. Recognize that this list can make you more anxious if you try to implement all suggestions at once. Pick those items which are more relevant to you and only focus on one or two at a time.

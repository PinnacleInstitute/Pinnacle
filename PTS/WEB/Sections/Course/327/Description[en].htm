Course Requirements

Students are required to read all course materials, respond to assignments and take quizzes over each section, participate in the class message board to share experiences, and take a final exam to demonstrate mastery of concepts.

Course Goals

The goals of this course include educating parents and child care providers about basic concepts in behavior management and offering suggestions and techniques that will help most children become better listeners and more respectful family members and citizens.

Grading Policy

Each lesson includes a pass/fail assignment that will help students apply information and a quiz to help students assess their own mastery of the material presented.  There is a final exam that covers important concepts.  Assignments are graded on a pass/fail basis and quizzes require a score of at least 70% to pass.

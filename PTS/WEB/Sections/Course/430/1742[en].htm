CSM Prospecting

 

Prospecting is the main motor or engine to building your business. The key is to know what works. Effective prospecting will increase your week of time and the quality.  Create a mantra. Consistently think about how you can increase your prospecting. How can I get better than I am right now?

 You are looking for business owners who want to increase their income, get a tax dollar benefit and are serious about doing something different to get different results.

 

  1. Networking
    1. Join other networking clubs

i.      Chambers
ii.      Optimist
iii.      Rotary
iv.      Lions
v.      Toastmaster

b. Create an agreement with the Chamber for every referral they send you that enrolls for class you will pay the chamber a $200.00 referral fee. Become a valuable resource for the chambers.

c.       Post articles in the chamber newsletters

d.      Be a member of everything possible.

e.       Be a volunteer. This is a great networking opportunity.

 

  1. Advertising

a.       Money makes money for your business

b.      Advertise in 3-4 papers

i.      Business opportunity section

ii.      Look up good ads on the CSM boards

c.       Put ads in surrounding areas

d.      Use Penny Saver papers

e.       Ask for weekend specials

f.        Ask for sister paper discounts

g.       Always advertise on Sunday

h.       Spend about $40-$60 a week

i.         Team members do their own ads

j.        If you do not get 5-7 leads change the ad

k.      Ads and your money work harder than you do.

l.         Advertise on the internet.

m.     Consumer ads talk about raising capital and helping grow businesses

n.       Create articles to be published in business journals, newsletters and local papers.

o.      Advertise in areas where we do not currently have regions.

p.      Advertise where you have family and friends

q.      Advertise in place you would like to go visit.

 

  1. Build a Referral Team

a.       Pre-qualifying meetings are lunches and breakfast meetings. Use these meetings to feed your preview.

b.      Build referral teams of 12-15 people. They do not need to come to meetings you ask them to always think about IBI when they meet someone if they would be a good fit for IBI.

c.       It is always the responsibility of the CSM to close the sale not of the Area Mangers or the Forum Representatives.

d.      Ask a retail store to post your event information and brochures for you. Make them a referral source.

i.      Pick 10-15 Retail establishments who will post your information. Buy nice Acrylic racks for posting your information and update the information weekly.

ii.      This adds another profit center to the business.

iii.      Pick retail establishments who service the type of people you are looking for

1.      Mail Box locations
2.      Coffee Shops
3.      Office supply stores
4.      Copy locations
5.      Restaurants who host networking events

    1. As soon as some one enrolls explain that they are now part owners of the club and the success on the club depends on the membership always increasing. Hand the new member a referral form to make a list of who they know. On the back of the form have a list of the cities we currently have offices in.
    2. Tell your reps the type of people you are looking for

i.      Plumbers
ii.      Accountants
iii.      Lawyers
iv.      Doctors
v.      Service providers

    1. Get 10 people to give you 50 names each and that is 500 people you have to work with.

 

  1. Language for potential prospecting

a.       Quality cost money, we are the best and cost you $5000.00 to get your income up

b.      Want more income you have to do something extraordinary

c.       To warp drive your business and speed things up is valuable

d.      Your language in getting people to show up has to be strong.

i.      Give them more than one reminder

1.      email
2.      cell phone
3.      fax maps and good directions

e.       If you are not asking them for their cell phone numbers and if you can send a map they are not committed to come. Ask them if you can have their cell phone number to call them and confirm their attendance. If they do not give it to you they are not coming.

f.        Ask for the deeper commitment. Are you really coming? I am going to tell the hotel I am expecting a certain number of guests. I need to know you will be there.

g.       Ask them, “What do you need from me to know for sure you are coming? “

 

 

  1. Closing the Sale

a.       If the person has a spouse or business partner get them to attend the preview so there is not excuse of having to talk things over before making the commitment. This way the partner/spouse has all the same information as your new prospect.

b.      Ask for their business get the order form in their hand. Give them a pen.

c.       Make a call for action. They will not enroll if you do not ask them to.

d.      Do not let the people attending the preview run out into the world of negativity with out making a commitment to enroll.

e.       If they do not enroll at class ask them when you can meet them to pick up the form.

f.        Tell them they will never be ready.

 

  1. Recruiting CSMs

a.       Get every area manager to be a Regional Manager.

b.      5 Regional Mangers could lead to 4-7K a month in income

c.       People will not stay if they are not getting paid.

d.      Do not wait until the Area Manager has used up their warm market to encourage them to be a CSM

e.       Ads should be designed to build CSMs in every region.

i. This stabilizes your region. Do this through advertising in the Help Wanted Sales section of your paper.

f.        Interview the prospects and them pass them to the home office for training.

g.       Participate in job fairs.

h.       Look for people who are interested in self employment sales careers.

i.         Do not spend time on training CSM, spend time on recruiting them and let the home office train them.

j.        Look for sales people.

k.      People you bring in may want to do it differently than you.

l.         Remember they are not your employees.

m.     Sr. CSM work for the newer managers. How can you make them more successful, how can you make them happy?

n.       Share cost and expenses and not the income.

o.      Bigger rooms come from bringing in new managers

p.      5 you thrive and 7 you are in heaven

 

  1. Working in Cooperation 

    1. Share with the other CSMs what works for you on the CSM boards.
    2. Bigger meetings consistently means more area managers and more CSMs.
    3. Always be in IBI Cooperation.
    4. Focus on the company as a whole
    5. Support the team leader as a whole.
    6. The new normal is cooperation
    7. Be supportive of the team leaders
    8. Respect the office of the position if you are not like minded with the person
    9. Be a model for the other CSMs to follow.

 

  1. Key tips

a.       5 and you thrive 7 and you are in heaven.

b.      Create a strategy plan.

    1. As you get busy start having group private meetings with qualified prospects.
    2. Never reduce your time in prospecting. Stop the things that keep you from talking to a new person.

e.       This business is built by one person who needs this product referring others who need the product.

f.        This is not a high volume business.

g.       Your first plateau is getting 5 to class. Then they come back and help in spreading the word. This leads to 15-20 consistently in the rooms.

h.       You make more money when you become a career giver.

i.         At the end of the day measure the amount of time you spent prospecting. Did you do anything to avoid prospecting?

j.        Make your best time of day your prospecting time of day.

k.      You should prospect at least 90 minutes a day.

l.         Focus on rhythm and systems.

m.     Having at least 3 people at pre-qualifying luncheons and breakfast leads to 6 coming to the preview.

n.       You have to invite 5-7 to have 3

o.      Stay focused and do not get distracted.

p.      Can not start up and stop. Do not close your retail store the first year you are open.

q.      Can not be involved in other businesses that will distract you.

r.        Be careful of building MIT’s while building your business.

s.       Get rid of amateur language.

t.        Increase your commitment.

u.       Start new sales techniques right away

v.       Post your wins on the CSM boards.

w.     Pray with the home office and other CSMs

i.      Pray that God brings you abundance and the prospects who are ready to receive.

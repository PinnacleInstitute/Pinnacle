This class is the foundation for all the other classes in this program.  You'll understand what Contrarian job hunting is all about and why you may find yourself resisting some of the concepts in the program.

Essentially, we will take all your assumptions about the way you're "supposed to" find a job, and throw them out the window.  In their place, you'll begin to learn the secrets of professional salespeople who know how to reach decision makers, get the meeting and come away with a "yes!"

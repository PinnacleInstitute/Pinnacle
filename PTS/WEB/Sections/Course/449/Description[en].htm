This class will be totally unlike any program you've taken or website or book you've read on job hunting.  You'll gain a new perspective and practical, learn a bevy of real world "how-to's" and finish the class feeling empowered and in control of your job search and your life.

 

In this class, you’ll learn:

 

·        why the “Traditional” system of relying on want ads, web sites, headhunters and networking actually works against you,

·        a new way to think and feel about job hunting that will have a dramatic and powerful effect on you and those you contact,

·        how to find, research and approach the key decision makers in companies you want to work for,

·        the seven myths of resume writing,

·        how to write a resume and cover letter that will make employers want to meet you (whether or not they are hiring),

·        why there is no such thing as “rejection,”

·        how to anticipate and handle objections, and

·        how to do proper follow-up.

 

You’ll also gain unique insights into what a job interview is really all about, and learn very simple but highly effective techniques to get an employer to want to hire you.  Have your resume and cover letter handy.

 

Handouts will be provided online in Adobe format.

Follow-up is your responsibility, not the employer's.  If you want to get hired, you have to call after sending in your material.  But what do you say?

What if the secretary won't put you through?

What if the decision maker answers the phone?

What if all you get is voice mail?

The worst thing you can do is make assumptions you're being dismissed or rejected, or simply give up out of frustration. 

This lesson will tell you what follow-up is all about.

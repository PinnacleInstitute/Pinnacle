Course Goals

The goal of this course is to tame the clutter demon which has taken over your office - whether you have a cubicle or corner window office downtown, or a home office, or just a work space at home where you pay bills.

Course Materials

No extra materials beyond those you'll find in any office are absolutely necessary. You may want to borrow or buy an automatic labeller. You will certainly want file folders and may decide to purchase an organizer-type notebook.

Grading Policy

You'll know if you've passed!

Expected Outcome: Employees to be on time.

What: Employee will be punctual and prepared for the day.

Process:

  • Enter chapel through Chapel Information door
  • Before reporting to your scheduled work station, clock in.
  • Report to work in appropriate attire, including nametag, daily schedule of weddings, and run slips.
  • Overtime has to be previously discussed/authorized by Supervisor.
  • If unable to report to work due to illness, notify Supervisor at least two hours prior to schedule shift. Leave a message on his or her phone and continue trying to reach a Supervisor to ensure shift is covered.
  • No loitering in other departments, unless providing lateral service.
  • Clock out for breaks, lunch, and end of shift.
  • Employees required to attend weekly training and department meetings.

Standard:

  • Punch in on time.
  • All employees to report to work station within five (5) minutes of scheduled start time.
  • Appearance to be professional and clean (i.e. no rips, stains, or missing buttons).
  • All employees wearing nametags.
  • Tardiness and no-shows will result in disciplinary action.
  • All overtime approved in advance.
  • Punch out on time.

Material:

  • Swipe in/out card
  • Nametag
  • Reports

Machines:

  • Time Clock
  • Computers

Environment:

  • Chapel Information
  • Floral
  • Chapels

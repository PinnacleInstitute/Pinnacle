Technical Requirements

No previous musical knowledge needed.

Course Requirements

Only a desire to learn how to read music.

Course Goals

To take the beginner through 15 stages that will lead to a complete knowledge of how to read music. Useful for anyone wanting to learn an instrument, join a choir, help pupils with their homework or to strengthen the musician who plays 'by ear' only. At step 15 all of the basic elements will have been introduced and the world of music can be seen with a new perspective.

Course Materials

A comfortable pencil with sharpener, a ruler, eraser, plain A4 paper and lined A4 paper.

Grading Policy

Each lesson and end of section will finish with a quiz to consolidate knowledge although the marks for them will not count towards the final course grade. Lesson 15 is a final test with the pass mark giving the course grade.

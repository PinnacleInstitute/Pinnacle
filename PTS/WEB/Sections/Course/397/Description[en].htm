Course requirements:  Participants must come with an open, inquiring mind, ready to examine what the term public relations means.  The fact is, each person and each company has public relations.  Whether good or bad is up to the management and the employees who produce the products or services.

Goal: To bring new awareness of public relations' importance to the success of the organization, and in the end, each member of the management and staff.

Materials: Outline for each lesson so that participants may make notes of their own in order to be able to apply what is learned and put it to use every day in their work and with their own personal publics.

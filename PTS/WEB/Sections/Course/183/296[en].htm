SELF MARKETING SUPPORT MATERIALS

 

The key to marketing success is the ability match product attributes to what the target customer “needs or wants”. The channels leading to potential opportunities tend to be overly focused and narrow and leave little room for unique capabilities to be perceived by those making selection decisions. Creative marketing communication techniques and materials greatly improve marketing success. The following resources facilitate this matching process.

 

NEEDS ASSESSMENT

 

When targeting a potential position opportunity, it is critical that the position description be very carefully analyzed. The primary requirements are usually presented and become clues to guide “self marketing” success. Consistently communicating the match at each phase of the evaluation process will continually move the skillful candidate up the selection curve. Remember that the demonstration of skills or capabilities through accomplishment statements is a very powerful marketing tool.

ANALYSIS CHECKLIST

 

Have most needs and/or wants been matched to candidate accomplishments?

 

Are needed skills clearly described in matching accomplishment statements?

 

Are matches supported by metrics whenever possible?

 

Has a clear link between candidate capabilities and position requirements been demonstrated?

 

TARGET OPPORTUNITIES

 

When presenting capabilities, it is very important to link skills to the needs/wants of those selecting candidates for evaluation. The cover letter or email message is the connecting vehicle that highlights the match. A consistent, accomplishment driven theme should be developed and reinforced through out the communication/evaluation process.

 

Remember to tell them what you are going to tell them, tell them and tell them what you told them.

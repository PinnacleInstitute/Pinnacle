Your Member Home Page is the first page you will see after logging into your new system.  This home page provides quick access to all your system tools.   

Menu Bar

You can always return to your member home page by clicking the menu option called "Home" at the top of your menu bar on the left side of most pages.

The options on the menu to the left may not match your options exactly.  Each user or membership level may have a different set of options available to them. 

You will learn more about the other menu options from the other user tutorial lesson that will follow.

Your menu includes several sections that can be collapsed and expanded by clicking the small up and down arrows to the right of each section title.

You can click the name of any menu item to to go directly to the desired functionality.

If you have a "Sign Out" section at the bottom of your menu, you can click it to change your logon name and password, or to sign out of the system.
 

 

Dashboard

Near the top of your Member Home Page are several large icons for easy access to your most frequent tasks.  You will learn more about customizing these icon options in a later lesson titled "Quick Access Icons".

Below the large icons is your dashboard.  It displays a quick preview and access to your most important information.  You may or may not have all these dashboard sections depending on your personal access options.  You can view a list of all your activities scheduled for today on the left side of your dashboard.  Each item in your "Today" list can be clicked to popup a window with detailed information about the item.  These items may be scheduled appointments, contact callbacks, prospect follow-ups, current goals, customer service activities, etc. 

You will learn more about each of these items in your "Today" list and the other sections on the right side of your dashboard in later lessons.  Each of the items on the right side of the dashboard are also clickable for quick access to the detailed information.

You can click the button at the bottom-right of your dashboard to view a summary of your membership information.

 

 

Quick Access Tools

The bottom half of your Member Home Page contains several additional quick access tools organized in various sections like Team Management, Training and Education, Sales and Marketing, and Business Support.  You may or may not have all these quick access tools depending on your personal access options. 

You will learn more about these quick access tools from the other user tutorial lesson that will follow.

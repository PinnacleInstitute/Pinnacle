Your Quick Access Icons at the top of your member home page provide easy access to your most frequently used functions.

You can edit your Quick Access Icons by first clicking the "My Info" menu option on the left side of the page in the Personal section.


 

  


At the top of the Member Profile, click the "Icons" link on the right side of the page. 

 

 

 

Select Icons

Enter a number next to each icon to specify the order for it to appear at the top of your member home page.  Leave the number set to zero to hide the icon.  If more than six icons have a number specified, a second row of Quick Access Icons will be displayed at the top of your member home page.  Click the "Update" button at the bottom of the page to save your selections.

 

 

After saving your Quick Access Icon selections, you will be returned to your member profile page.  

Click the "Home" menu option on the top-left side of the page

to view your selected Quick Access Icons across the top of your member home page.

 

 

 

 

You have learned how your Quick Access Icons at the top of your member home page provide easy access to your most frequently used functions.

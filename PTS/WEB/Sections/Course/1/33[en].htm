You can predefine sets of goals as Success Tracks for your team members or your customers.  


 

Custom Success Tracks

 

Success Tracks are predefined sets of activities to accomplish any business process.  A Success Track defines the steps to follow, the recommended timeline to accomplish the steps, all support resources for each step, and accountability and recognition for the team member.

Success Tracks may be defined for a team member to accomplish any business process or for their own personal or professional development.  Some examples may include setting up your business, doing a trade show, monthly prospecting goals, a retailing process, a management process, a leadership process, etc.

Customer Success Tracks may be defined as processes to be accomplished in servicing a customer. Some examples may include initial customer follow-up or ongoing maintenance follow-up.

You can define a new success track by clicking the "New Success Track" option in the upper-right corner.  You can edit an existing success track by clicking the icons following the name in the list. 

 

 

 

Edit Success Track

Click the first pencil icon following the name in the list to edit the success track.  The name and description of the success track are displayed at the top of the page.  Click the "Add New Task" button to add a new task for this success track.

A Success Track can have an unlimited number of steps and more complex steps may have sub tasks.  Each step or task is a goal that includes a name, description, projected completion date and optional reminder date.

The tasks or displayed in alphabetic order.  Enter a number in the task name field to control the task sequence if necessary.

Click the name of the task in the list below to edit its sub-tasks if desired.  You can click the edit icon following the task name to edit the task details.


 

Success Track Timeline

You can define the projected completion dates for each step of the Success Track. When a success track is added, the projected completion dates for each step are automatically set based on the creation date of the Success Track. 

For example, if the Success Track was created on 12/1/2009, and task #1 has a projected completion date of 12/3/2009, and the member adds the success track on 12/15/2009, the member's newly created task #1 will have a projected completion date of 12/17/07 (2 days after the creation date).

The member's success track email reminders are also automatically set based on the Success Track create date and the task's email reminder date.

 

 

Success Track Resources

All the necessary resources for a success track can be defined with each step of the process. A step may include detailed instructions, sales scripts, reference materials, downloadable documents,  or additional tools. 

Each of these resources can be created in your Resource Center with the Information Pages and the Uploaded Documents options. References (links) can be placed in the description of each task. The task description supports any URL links to any resource on the Internet including links to your information pages and uploaded documents. 

 

 

Success Track Reminders

You can also define the reminder dates for each step of the Success Track.  If a member's step is not complete, the system will send and email reminder to the member on the reminder date. The reminder dates are automatically set when the member adds the success track, in the same way the projected completion dates are set. The reminder date may be any number of days before or on the projected completion date.

 

   

Success Track Calendar

If you select your calendar option to display goals, each of your success track steps will be displayed on the corresponding projected completion date.

 

  

Joining Success Tracks

A member can join a success track from their "My Goals" page. In the upper right hand corner of the page is a link named "Join Success Track".  When a success track is joined, each step of the success track is added as a goal for the member, and will appear in their goal list and on their calendar. 

 

 

Join Success Track

A member can view Success Tracks to join that have been predefined by the company, by their mentor or by any other member.  The "Company Tracks" option is selected by default.  The list below will contain all the selected Success Tracks to join.  They can select a Success Track from the list and click the "Preview" button to preview the goals and tasks contained in the selected Success Track before joining it.


The name of new goal to be created from the Success Track will be set to the name of the Success Track by default.  They can edit the new goal name to be whatever you would like.  They can click the "Join" button when they are ready to create a new set of goals from the selected Success Track.

They can select the "Mentor Tracks" option to view a list of Success Tracks predefined by their mentor.

They can view a list of Success Tracks predefined by any other member, by entering the member's number in the textbox following the "Tracks for Member #" option.  Click the option, and the Success Track list below will be populated with the selected Success Tracks.  The textbox will be defaulted to their mentor's number.  

They can request the member number from any member willing to share their Success Tracks with them.  If they want to view the Success Tracks from a different member, they can enter the other member's number in the textbox and click the option again, even if it is already selected.  

  

  

Customer Success Tracks

Customer Success Tracks are used in the Customer Management System.  On the detailed customer page you can click the "Follow-up" folder tab to add a Customer Success Track or to add a single follow-up goal.

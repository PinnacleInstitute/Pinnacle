You can define custom sales campaigns to track the steps of your specific sales processes.  


 

Custom Sales Campaigns

A Sales Campaign defines each of the steps for a sales process.  You can create a custom Sales Campaign for each of your specific sales processes.  Click the "Add New Sales Campaign" option in the upper-right corner to create a new sales campaign.  Click the pencil icon following the name in the list to edit an existing sales campaign.   

 

  

Add New Sales Campaign

Enter the name of your new sales campaign and a sequence number for the order you want it to be displayed in the list.

You can copy an existing sales campaign as a starting point for your new sales campaign.  Select an existing sales campaign to copy from the list at the bottom of the page.  The number for the selected sales campaign will be placed in the text box to the left of the list.  You can click the "Preview" button to view the selected sales campaign to confirm that it is the one that you want to copy. 

If you do not select an existing sales campaign to copy, a new empty sales campaign will be created and you will need to add each of the desired sales steps.

Click the "Add" button to create your new Sales Campaign.

 

 

 

  

Edit Sales Campaign

Click the pencil icon following the name in the list to edit the existing sales campaign.  You can edit the name, sequence and sales steps.  Each of the steps for the sales campaign are listed near the bottom of the page.

Click the "Add New Sales Step" option in the upper-right corner above the list to add a new sales step.  Click the pencil icon following the name in the list to edit the existing sales step.

 

 

 

  

Edit Sales Step

Enter a sales step name, description and step number.  You can optionally specify the recommended number of hours that his step should take to accomplish.

If you want to track the date that this step was accomplished, enter a value from 1 to 10 for the milestone date.

If you want this sales step to appear on the Sales Board Report, check the Sales Board checkbox near the bottom of the page.  

Enter the average conversion rate for prospects that reach this step.  For example; An average of one out of five prospects that reach step #1 are eventually closed (20% or .20).  This conversion rate is used by the Sales Board Report to calculate the actual value of your sales board as a percentage of the potential value of a prospect.

 

 

  

Sales Campaign Usage

The sales campaign is available for selection at the top of the Contact detail page in the Contact Manager. 

CONTACT MANAGER

 

The sales campaign is also displayed near the top of the Prospect detail page in the Sales Management System.  You can click the "Change" button following the sales campaign name to select a different sales campaign.

SALES MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

 

The sales campaign is also displayed at the top in the blue bar on the Customer detail page in the Customer Management System.

CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

 

In the Sales Management System the selected sales campaign is used to track the status of the prospect in the Sales Status folder tab.  All steps of the sales campaign are listed in the Sales Status list box along with the standard steps of New, Fallback, Closed and Dead.

All steps that have a milestone value from one to ten are displayed in the list of milestone dates along with the standard milestone dates.  The sales step description is displayed to the right of the date text box.

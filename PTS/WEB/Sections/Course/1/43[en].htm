You can publish your own personal News Letters to share valuable information with other interested members and prospects.


 

Publish Newsletters

A list of all your Published Newsletters will be displayed for your review and editing.  The Newsletter status is displayed on the right side of the list.  The company may decide to feature cetain newsletters for the other members.  If your newletter has been selected as a featured newletter it will be designated in your list,  You can also see how many members and prospects have subscribed to your newsletter.  You can click the pencil  icon to the right of the Newsletter name to edit the newsletter options. 

Click the "Add New Newsletter" link in the upper-right corner to add a new newsletter.

 


 

The members will be able to view and subscribe to all available newletters if they have the newletter option enabled.  The user tutorial lesson on Newsletters describes the details on how to do this.

 

 

Add Newsletter

Enter a name for your new Newsletter.  You can change the status to Ready later after you have created your first newsletter issue.  Enter a description for your newsletter that will help others better understand what your newletter will offer them.

Click the "Add" button to create your new newsletter.

 

Edit Newsletter

In your list of Newsletters, click the pencil  icon to the right of the Newsletter name to edit the newsletter options.

You can edit the newsletter name and set the status to active when you are ready to make your newsletter available for subscriptions.  

Your newsletter issues are displayed at the bottom of the page.  They are displayed with the most recent issue at the top of the list.  

You can edit the page options and content at the bottom of the page.  You can click the pencil  icon to the right of the issue name to edit the issue options. 

Click the "Add New Issue" link in the upper-right corner above the issue list to add a new issue.

 


 


  

Add Newsletter Issue

Enter a name for your new issue and click the "Add" button.

 

  

Edit Newsletter

You can click the pencil  icon to the right of the issue name in the issue list to edit the issue options. 

You can edit  the issue name, status, send date, sending email address, subject of the issue and the issue message.  Click the "Update" button in the upper-right corner or on the bottom of the page.  The "Cancel" button will return to the previous page without saving your changes.

You can edit your issue message with the WYSIWYG (What-You-See-Is-What-You-Get) Editor to apply formatting similar to a word processor.  You can use the three editor toolbars to select the font style, color and size, format and align the text, insert your images, as well as many other functions.  

You can also insert images into your newsletter.  You must first upload your images to the system by clicking the "Upload Images" option in the upper-right corner above the editor.  After uploading the image, the full URL for the image will be displayed in purple new the bottom of the upload page.  Select the URL with your mouse and copy it (Control Key + C).  

Next in the WYSIWYG editor, place the cursor were you want the new image to be inserted.  Click the image icon on the right side of the 2nd toolbar.   Paste the image URL into the URL field by pressing the Control Key + V.  Your image will be displayed below.  You may also want to modify the width and height of your image,  and specify vertical and/or horizontal spacing around your image to separate your text from the image.  Finally, select the desired alignment option for your image (usually left or right).

When you are ready to publish your newsletter issue, change the status to "Ready" and set the "Send Date" to the date you want the system to email this issue to all the subscribers.
 

 
  
Replaceable Tags

You can insert tags that will be replaced with the recipient's firstname {firstname}  or their last name {lastname}.  These two tags can be placed in the issue subject or anywhere in the issue message.

 

Preview Issue 

You can preview your newsletter issue to confirm that it is ready for publication.  At the bottom of your issue page enter an email address to send the test issue to. Also enter a firstname and lastname to be used for the replaceable tags.

Click the "Test Email" button to send the issue to the test email address.  Click the "Preview Email" button to view a popup window displaying the issue.




  

  

You have learned how you can publish your own personal News Letters to share valuable information with other interested members and prospects.

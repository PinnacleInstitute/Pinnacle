You can manage your business expenses to accurately account for your allowable tax deductions.  When done properly, your allowable tax deductions can save you significantly on your owed taxes.  These tax savings can significantly offset the expenses of owning your own business. 



Business Expense Management

Your Expense Manager allows you to manage your tax deductible business expenses.  Your Expense Manager is only a tool for tracking expenses and does not replace the professional advice of a tax accountant.  Work with your tax accountant to get further clarification on your allowable tax deductions.

The very first time you access your Expense Manager, you will be prompted to enter your current year tax deduction options.  You can access your tax options by clicking the "Expense Options" link in the upper right corner.

 

 

  

Expense Options

Your expense options are specified for each tax year.  Each tax year option is listed below and is editable by clicking the pencil icon following the year.  You can add a new expense option by clicking the "Add New Expense Option" in the upper right corner.

 

 

 

 

  

Edit Expense Option

Your expense options for the specified year determine how deductions will be calculated for your vehicle and home office usage.  You can also enter notes to document your assumptions in setting your options.  If you change your options after expense items have already been added, you can recalculate your expense tax deductions by clicking the "Recalculate Expenses" button at the bottom of the page.  Make sure you saved your changes first by clicking the "Update" button before recalculating expenses.

 

 

Expense Manager

From your Expense Manager you can add new expenses by clicking the "Add New Expense" option at the top of the page.  Click the pencil icon following the expense amount to edit an existing expense.  The small icon to the left of the expense item identifies the type of expense as defined by the legend at the bottom of the page.

 

  

 

 

 

 

 

Add New Expense

Your business expenses are organized into five major categories.  Select the appropriate category for your new expense.  You will then be taken to the expense detail page where you can select an appropriate sub-category.

 

 

 

Edit Expense

Your expense detail page allows you to edit the sub-category, date and expense amount.  Other text boxes may be available for entry depending on the type of expense.  You will always want to document your expense at the bottom of the page for tax auditing purposes.

You can optionally attach receipts to your expense item by clicking the "Receipts" button at the bottom of the page.  The receipts must be in an electronic format by being scanning in or otherwise digitized.  If receipts have been uploaded with the expense item, the number will be displayed on the expense button.     

 

 

 

Expense Help

You can get additional help on allowable tax deductions by clicking the expense categories in the legend at the bottom of the page.  You can also click the help icon at the top of the expense detail page.  Remember to work with your tax accountant to get further clarification on your allowable tax deductions.

 

Expense Logs

You can download printable expense logs from the expense help pages for each of the five major categories.

 

 

Expense Report

You can view your expense report from the Expense Manager by clicking the "Expense Report" option in the upper-right corner.  You can also access your expense report from the expense option page for each tax year.  Specify the date range directly or select an option from the list box for a preset date range.

Your expenses will be summarized by major category.  You can click the report icon to the right of each expense category to view a detail listing of expenses for that category.

 

 

 

 

 

You have learned how you can manage your business expenses to accurately account for your allowable tax deductions.

Success Tracks

Success Tracks are sets of goals and tasks predefined to accomplish a specific business purpose.  Success Tracks can be predefined by the company or by any member, including your mentor, that has the Custom Success Tracks option enabled.

Some examples of Success Tracks include, getting started processes for new salespersons, monthly sales activity goals, special marketing programs, etc.

You can join a Success Track from your Goal Manager by clicking the "Join Success Track" link in the upper-right corner. 

 

 

Join Success Track

Your can view Success Tracks to join that have been predefined by the company, by your mentor or by any other member.  The "Company Tracks" option is selected by default.  The list below will contain all the selected Success Tracks to join.  Select a Success Track from the list and click the "Preview" button to preview the goals and tasks contained in the selected Success Track before joining it.


The name of new goal to be created from the Success Track will be set to the name of the Success Track by default.  You can edit the new goal name to be whatever you would like.  Click the "Join" button when you are ready to create a new goal from the selected Success Track.

Select the "Mentor Tracks" option to view a list of Success Tracks predefined by your mentor.

To view a list of Success Tracks predefined by any other member, enter the member's number in the textbox following the "Tracks for Member #" option.  Click the option, and the Success Track list below will be populated with the selected Success Tracks.  The textbox will be defaulted to your mentor's number.  Request the member number from any member willing to share their Success Tracks with you.  If you want to view the Success Tracks from a different member, enter the other member's number in the textbox and click the option again, even if it is already selected.  

 

NOTE: You can also create a new goal from any existing goal if you know the goal number displayed at the top of the Goal Details page or the Goal Edit page.  Go to the "Add New Goal" page and enter the existing goal number in the "Copy Goal #" textbox at the bottom of the page.

Create Success Tracks

Your can create your own Success Tracks for your personal use or to share with others if you have the "Custom Success Track" option enabled.  You can learn more about how to do this in the "Custom Success Track" lesson later in the User Tutorial.  If this lesson is not in your User Tutorial, then you do not have the option enabled.

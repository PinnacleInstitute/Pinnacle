Your Lead Manager helps you organize and track your potential prospects that have not yet expressed an interest in your offer.


My Leads

Your Lead Manager allows you to manage your potential prospects, sometimes referred to as suspects.   You can search for an existing contact or add new leads ten at a time.   
   
 

 

Add New Leads

Click the "Add New Leads" link in the upper-right corner of the Lead Manager to add up to ten new leads at a time.  Enter their name, phone, and email address.  You can also prioritize them as A, B or C to designate them as a Hot, Warm or Cold lead.  You can use any prioritizing method that works best for you.  The priority text box can hold up to four numbers or characters.  Priority is one of the fields you can search and sort by.  You can also add a note for each new lead to describe how you know them or where you met them.

  

 

Search Contacts 

 

You can search for an existing contact by any of the fields in the "Search By" list box.  Enter the text you want to search for in the "Find" text box and click the "Go" button.  The search text does not need to be an exact match.  The system will search for all contacts that start with the entered search text in the selected field.  If you search by the Contact Name, the system will list all contacts where the search text is anywhere within the Name field. 

The search results are also sorted by the selected search field.  If you enter nothing in the search text box, all contacts will be listed sorted by the search field.  If you select the Entry Date or the Callback Date search fields with no search text, the leads will be listed with the most current dates at the top of the list.  This is a handy way of listing your newest leads. 

You can further filter your list of leads by specifying a status in the second list box. New Leads have no status yet, Active Leads have a status of 1st, 2nd, or 3rd contact , Live Leads have any status except Dead, and the remaining status selections match the Lead's specific status.

The selected leads are listed twenty leads at a time.  On the right side of the result list you can click the "Next Page" and "Previous Page" links to view the next or previous twenty leads.  If the list is too long, you may want to be more specific in your search criteria.

When the system returns a list of selected leads, click the name of the lead to view and edit the detailed contact information.  

  

  

Edit Leads

You can provide more detailed information about your leads including a specific sales campaign and the contact type.  You can also track the status of the lead as you work with them in an attempt to convert them into a prospect.  You can track how many contacts you've made and if you have a callback date and time set.  You can also designate them as a "Dead" lead if they've given you an absolute "No".  

You can track multiple phone numbers and the best time to call, in addition to their mailing address.  You can enter up to 500 characters of notes and you can insert timestamps by clicking the small clock icon to distinguish multiple notes. 

You can specify the source of the lead, for example if you collected business cards at a networking meeting you can enter up to 20 characters in the searchable source field. 

 

You can enter a callback date and time at the lead's request or per your schedule.  If a callback date is specified, the lead will automatically appear on your calendar and on your Home Page list of Today's Activities when the date arrives.

You can click the shortcut icon at the top of the page to the right of the contact name to create shortcut on your member home page to the lead.

  

 

Move Leads to Prospects

When you find an interested lead you can make them a prospect by moving them to the Sales Management System.  You will learn more about managing prospects in the Sales Management lesson of these User Tutorials. 

Before you can move a lead you must have a name and an email address entered.  If you click the "Move To Prospect" button a new prospect record will be created with all the lead information and the lead will no longer appear in your Lead Manager.  You will be returned to the Lead Manager search page.  If you click the "Move & Go To Prospect" button you will be taken directly to the new prospect detail record in the Sales Management System.  

 

  

Email Contacts

You can send a new email to the contact and you can view emails already sent to this contact by clicking the "Send New Email" and "View Email" links in the upper-right corner of the Contact Detail Page.

  

 

Distribute Leads

If you need to, you can share your leads with other members.  On the Lead Manager search page, click the "Distribute Leads" link in the upper-right corner.  You will first be asked to enter the member number of the person to receive the leads.  Click the "Lookup Member" button to view the member's name and confirm that you entered the correct member number. 

 

You will be able retrieve leads to distribute using the same criteria as the Lead Manager search page.  All leads matching the search criteria will be listed rather than just twenty at a time.  

Each lead will have a checkbox next to the lead's name.  You can click the checkboxes individually or you can check and uncheck all or ten leads at a time.  When you have selected all the leads to distribute to the specified member, you can click the "Distribute" button.  You will see a message box confirming the number of leads that were transferred to the specified member.  The selected leads will no longer appear in your Lead Manager.

An email with all the distributed leads is sent to the member distributing the leads and to the member receiving the distributed leads.

The distributed leads will appear in the receiving member's Lead Manager.  The Lead detail page will display the member number that distributed the lead and the date and time they did so.

 
 
   

 

   

 

Export Leads

You can export leads from your Lead Manager search page by clicking on the blue arrow icon to the right of the "Go" button.  A new window will display a list of all the leads for the specified criteria in the Lead Manager search page.  You can copy and paste the displayed leads into another program like Microsoft Excel.

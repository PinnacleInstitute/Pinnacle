Mission  

Your new system was designed to help you build and retain a successful sales team and a loyal customer base.  This mission describes two key objectives, Building and Retaining.

Objectives

BUILDING

  

A proper understanding of your target market is necessary for the effective use of your resources regardless of whether you are retailing products and services or recruiting new sales team partners. 

  

1. You must know what problems you can solve in order to determine if you can meet the needs of the prospect and how to best do so. Potential customers may have various problems or needs that you can fulfill.  Potential sales team partners may also have various needs including part-time income, full-time income, dream-income, more time, or a desire to help others. 

2. When all the possible solutions are known you can better identify the types of prospects that may have a need for your solution. They may include people with health problems, convenience needs or productivity needs.  They might also be potential sales partners that are underpaid or under-appreciated employees or over-worked managers and executives. 

3. A good understanding of your target niches helps you identify where they congregate in order for you to get your message in front of them.  When you know where they are you can focus your advertising and promotional activities toward them.

4. You only have a few seconds to get their attention with the right emotionally charged short message that hits their hot buttons. Lead with the primary benefit that directly addresses their need.  Don't say to much here, just get their attention.

5. After you get their attention, You only have a few minutes to get their interest with a benefit laden value proposition in order to get them to take the next step by saying, “That’s interesting, I’d like to know more”.
   

The 3 Step Prospecting Process

Acquire Interested Prospects: they may come from your warm market of people you already know directly or indirectly, or they may come from your cold market of people you have not yet met.

Qualify Serious Prospects: you must develop a relationship with your interested prospects to determine how serious they are and whether or not they would be a good customer or sales partner for you.

Close Committed Prospects:  a serious prospect must make a commitment to purchase or join your sales team, and you must make a commitment to provide excellent service to them as a customer or a new sales partner.

 

Your new system was designed specifically to support these principles of targeted marketing.  The sales and marketing tools provide the programs for attracting the right prospects and ensuring an optimal use of your resources.

Core Principles of World-Class Organizations

The Gallup research found that the effectiveness of an organization  is a direct result of its ability to engage its members.  World-class organizations are five times more effective than average organizations.  Every member of an effective organization needs the following key elements.

Friendship

A friend to take them by the hand and show them the ropes 
A friend to encourage them and set realistic expectations
A friend to help them know they are a valued member
A friend to champion their acknowledgement and recognition

Responsibility

A well defined job and an accountability
All necessary training to complete the job successfully
All necessary tools to complete the job successfully
An opportunity to succeed

Development

An understanding of their strengths and weaknesses
Regular opportunities to experience personal growth
Regular opportunities to experience professional growth
Regular feedback on their progress

 

Your new system was designed with these core values in mind.  The team leadership tools provide the programs and the environment to foster these key elements of friendship, responsibility and development.

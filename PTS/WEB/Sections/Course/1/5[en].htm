Your Member Profile allows you to view and update your personal information and user options.


 

Member Profile

At the top of your profile page is displayed your unique member number.  Your member number is also used as your mentoring number, which you will learn about in the "Mentoring" lesson.  You can also view the last date and time you accessed the system and when you first enrolled on the system.

You can change your optional company name if you have a personal company.  If you leave it blank it will default to your last and first name.  You can also specify two email addresses for system notifications.  The first email address is for general notifications.  The second one is a special email address for prospecting and mentoring notifications.  You will learn more about this second email address below.

You can enter up to three telephone numbers for the best number to reach you in the day or night time and your mobile number.  You can enter multiple optional mailing addresses for mailing, billing, shipping or other purposes.  

You can upload a picture of yourself by clicking the "Upload" button.  The image file can be a .gif or .jpg file and the file size must be less than 15K.  The picture doesn't need to be any larger than about 100 by 125 pixels.  You can use the free Microsoft Paint program to edit your image and save it as a .jpg file.   
 

 

Quick Email Address

The system uses your quick email address to send all mentoring and prospecting notices.  Mentoring notices include when a new mentor is selected by the member and when the member has an accomplishment that the mentor has requested to be notified about.  These include signing into the system, completing a goal, completing a class, completing an assessment, completing a certification, closing a prospect, etc.

The quick email address is the same as the primary email address by default.  The quick email address can be used to receive text messages by changing it to a cell phone email address to receive the mentoring and prospecting notices.  The format for the cell phone email address varies per provider as outlined below. Email Signature

You will be able to edit your email signature only if you have the Contact Manager, Sales Systems or the Custer Service System enabled.

You can edit your email signature on the bottom half of the page.  You can use the WYSIWYG (What-You-See-Is-What-You-Get) Editor to apply formatting similar to a word processor.  You can use the three editor toolbars to select the font style, color and size, format and align the text, insert your image in your signature, as well as many other functions.  

To insert your image, select the picture icon near the end of the second toolbar.  In the popup window, click the "Member" folder icon.  Scroll down the file list searching for the image file with your member number as the filename. When you select a filename it will be displayed on the left side of the popup window.  If this is the correct image, click the "OK" button to return to the editor.

You may want to center align your newly inserted image with your contact information below it by selecting everything with the mouse and clicking the small center-align icon near the middle of the second toolbar. 

You can insert a new line immediately following the previous line by pressing the [shift]+[enter] keys together.  

You will learn how to create more custom signatures to be used for specific purposes in the later lesson on "Custom Signatures".

Your Meeting Planner will provide you with advanced functionality to plan a meeting or party with invitations, guest lists and RSVP tracking.   


 

Meeting Planner

Your Meeting Planner displays a list of all your meetings that you have designated on your calendar as appointments with planned meetings.  New meetings must first be created as new appointments on your calendar.  You will learn how to create a new meeting below.

You can click the name of the meeting plan to edit the host information, meeting invitation, guest list and meeting notes. 

 

 

Add New Meeting Plan

To add a new meeting from the Meeting Planner you can click the link near the top of the page called "Click Here to schedule an appointment for a new meeting".  This will take you to your calendar.  You can also go directly to your calendar from your member home page.

From your Calendar, click the "Add New Appointment" icon in the upper-right corner of the page.  Enter the new appointment information in the popup window and select the "Meeting Planner" checkbox near the bottom-right of the page.  

Click the "Add" button when you are ready to create your new appointment.  The new appointment will be added to your calendar and the page will be refreshed with a new "Plan Meeting" button at the bottom of the page. 

 

 

Meeting Host

Enter the name and email address of the host for your new meeting.  The host may be yourself or someone else who has volunteered to host your meeting or party.  Click the "Add" button to add the new meeting host.

 

 

Plan Your Meeting

You are now ready to start creating your meeting invitation and your guest list.  This can be done by you and/or your meeting host.  Your host can access your meeting plan outside of the system with the Host Access URL and Access Code at the bottom of the page.  You must also select the "trained host" checkbox to indicate that you have trained the host on how to use the meeting planner before the host will be granted access.   

You can completely remove this meeting plan by clicking the "Delete" button.  The original appointment will not be deleted, only the meeting plan. You will be prompted to confirm that you really want to delete the meeting plan.

Click the large icons near the bottom of the page to start creating your meeting invitation, guest list and meeting notes.

 

 

Host Access and Training

After you have trained your meeting host, you can provide him or her with the Host Access URL and Access Code at the bottom of your meeting Plan.  They will be presented with a page similar to the Meeting Plan above.  

The following training can be provided for your meeting host.  It is also available on the meeting planner page for your host by clicking the Tutorial icon .

Click Here to view the training for your meeting host.

 

 

Meeting Invitation

Enter your meeting location, address and special message for your meeting invitation.  Near the top of the page you can select from many different themes for your meeting invitation.  Click the "Preview" button to the right of the Theme list box to view what the invitation will look like with the selected theme and entered information.

You can enter your invitation message with the WYSIWYG (What-You-See-Is-What-You-Get) Editor to apply formatting similar to a word processor.  You can use the two editor toolbars to select the font style, color and size, format and align the text, as well as many other functions.  You will want to coordinate any custom formatting with the selected theme.  

You can insert a new line immediately following the previous line by pressing the [shift]+[enter] keys together.  

Click the "Update" button at the bottom of the page when you are ready to save your changes and return to your meeting plan.

Guest List

Your Guest List will be initially displayed as follows.  To start adding up to ten guests at a time click the "Add New Guests" button at the bottom of the page.

You can also copy a guest list from another meeting by clicking the "Copy Guest List" button.  You will need the Access Code from the bottom of the meeting Plan that you want to copy guests from.  Your host will not have this copy option.

 

 

Add New Guests

To add up to ten guests at a time, enter their first name, last name and email address below.  All three fields are required and empty rows will not be added.  Click the Add button when you are ready to add your new guests.     

 

 

Guest List Statistics

After you have added some guests you will be able to view your guest list statistics.  You will be able to see the total number of guests, the number of new guests that have not been sent an email invitation, the number of guests that have been sent an email invitation but have not yet responded, and the number of guests that have responded and what their response was.

To send an email invitation to all new guests, click the "Send New Invitations Now" button at the bottom of the list.  Clicking the "Send All Invitations Now" button will resend an email invitation to all guests.  This is handy for a follow-up reminder as your meeting approaches.  

You can remove guests from your list by selecting the checkbox to the far right of each guest to be removed and clicking the "Delete Checked" button at the bottom of the page.

Click the name of a guest to edit the detail information and status.

   

 

Edit Guest

You can edit your guest's name, email and status.  Reset the status back to "New" to resend a new email invitation.  You can also track if the guest actually attended the meeting and if you generated any sales from the guest.

 

 

 

Guest Invitation

Your guest will receive an email link to the following invitation from the meeting host.  They will be prompted to RSVP to your invitation.  They can also view a map of the meeting address if your selected the "View Map" option on your invitation. 

  

 

    

 

Meeting Notes

From you Meeting plan you can also keep an unlimited number of meeting notes to help you organize and document your plans for later debriefing and improving on future meetings.

You can be Personally Mentored by a qualified leader that can take you by the virtual hand and show you how to be successful.


 

Your Mentor

Your mentor will be an invaluable resource for your success and should be someone that you trust with your confidential information.  They will be able to review all your information including your calendars, goals, contacts, prospects, customers, assessments, courses, and those you mentor.

Your mentor may request to be automatically notified when you have an accomplishment including when you complete a class, complete an assessment, complete a certification, complete a goal, close a sale or access the system.

Your system may assign your mentor or it may allow you to select your own personal mentor.  If your options allow you to select a mentor you will see a "My Mentor's #" text box in the upper right corner of your mentoring page.  The very first thing you will want to do in your new system is to select your mentor.  Your mentor is typically the person that is supporting you and who you usually go to with questions.  Ask them if they would be willing to be your mentor.  They will need to be using the system and must have the option enabled to mentor others.  If they agree to be your mentor ask them for their mentoring number.  Their mentoring number is displayed near the top of their mentoring page following their name.

Enter their mentoring number in the upper-right corner of your mentoring page and click the "Update" button.  The page will be refreshed and your new mentor will be displayed.  The system will also send an email to the mentor to let them know that you've selected them as their mentor.  You can view your mentor's contact information by clicking the preview icon following your mentor's name.

You may have multiple mentors over time, but only one at a time.  Enter the new mentor's number and click the "Update" button to change your mentor.    

  

 

Mentor Communication

You will want to communicate with your mentor regularly.  Your system enables you to keep an unlimited number of mentoring notes.  You and your mentor can both leave mentoring notes for each other with advice and feedback.  Each note displays the date, time and who posted the note.

When your mentor leaves a new note for you it will appear in your list with a red note indicator.  Your Member Home Page will also display a red flashing mentoring note notice on your dashboard.   

Your mentoring notes are listed in chronological order with the most recent notes at the top.  You can edit notes that you have posted by clicking the pencil icon at the end of the note.  To add a new mentoring note, click the  "Add New Note" icon in the upper-right corner of the Notes list.

 

 

Add New Note

You can enter up to 1000 characters in each new note.  The current date and time are automatically set in the date field above the note and can be changed if necessary. If you want the note to always stay at the top of the list, you can select the checkbox called "Pin to the top of the list" found below the Notes text box.

  

 

Printable Notes

You can view a complete list of printable mentoring notes by clicking the "Printable Notes" link in the upper-right corner of the Notes list.

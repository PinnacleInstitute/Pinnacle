Your Customer Management System helps you manage the ongoing relationships with your valued customers.


Customer Management

Your system includes a professional Customer Management System similar to corporate Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems like SalesForce.com.

  

 

Search Customers 

 

You can search for an existing Customer by any of the fields in the "Search By" list box.  Enter the text you want to search for in the "Find" text box and click the "Go" button.  The search text does not need to be an exact match.  The system will search for all Customers that start with the entered search text in the selected field.  If you search by the Customer Name or Description, the system will list all Customers where the search text is anywhere within the Name or Description field respectively. 

The search results are also sorted by the selected search field.  If you enter nothing in the search text box, all Customers will be listed sorted by the search field. 

You can further filter your list of Customers by specifying a Campaign and / or Customer Type in the  two list boxes below the Search fields.  The Campaign and the Customer Type are carried over from the Sales Management System to help you organize your Customers.

The selected Customers are listed twenty Customers at a time.  On the right side of the result list you can click the "Next Page" and "Previous Page" links to view the next or previous twenty Customers.  If the list is too long, you may want to be more specific in your search criteria.

When the system returns a list of selected Customers, click the name of the Customer to view and edit the detailed contact information.

  

 

Adding Customers

Customers are not added directly into the Customer System.  They are first entered as a prospect in the Sales System.  When you close a prospect they are automatically visible in your Customer System.

 

 

Edit Customer

A summary of the Customer information is displayed near the top of the page in the light blue bar.  It includes the Customer's number, sales campaign, customer type and the date they became a customer.

 

You can edit the Customer's name, category and priority.  The priority textbox can contain up to four letters or numbers to support any prioritization system that best meets your needs.  For example; you could use A, B or C;  OR  1, 2 or 3;  OR  1a, 1b, 1c, 2a, 2b, 2c, 3a, 3b or 3c; etc.

When you are done reviewing or changing the Customer's information you can click the "Update" button to save your changes and stay on the detail Customer page, or click the "Update & Exit" button to save your changes and return to the Customer System, or click the "Return" button to return to the Customer System without saving your changes.  

You can review the prospect information for the customer in the Sales System before they became a customer by clicking the prospect icon in the upper-right corner of the page following the "Return" button. 

Additional Customer information is organized in several folder tabs as described below.    

 

 

Customer Contact Information

You can keep track of the Customer's contact information including the primary contact and their title, email and phone numbers, as well as the Customer's address and website.  Additional contact information can be tracked in the Customer's description or notes.

  

 

  

Customer Description

The Customer's description can contain up to 2000 characters to track any additional information.  You can also enter unlimited additional information in the Customer's notes.  

 

 

 

Customer Notes

You can enter an unlimited number of Customer notes for up to 1000 characters each.  The system will display the date, time and author of the note.  You can edit any notes you added by clicking the pencil icon following the note.  You cannot edit notes posted by others or by the system.  All notes are listed in chronological order except for notes that are explicitly pinned to the top of the list.  This is handy for reference information.   

Click the "Add New Note" link in the upper-right corner of the notes list to add a new note.

 

 

    

Customer Follow-up

Your Customer Management System provides the ability to define follow-up tasks for supporting and servicing your customers.  

Click the "Add New Follow-up Goal" link in the upper-right corner of the Follow-up list to add a new goal to follow-up with your customer.  Click the "Add New Follow-up Track" link to add a new Success Track (i.e. predefined set of goals) to follow-up with your customer.  

 

You will learn more about creating customer success tracks in the Custom Success Tracks lesson in the User Tutorial.  If this lesson is not available in your User Tutorial you don't have access to this option.

You can also copy any existing goal by entering its goal # at the bottom of the "Add New Goal" page.

Your Customer Follow-up goals will automatically appear on your calendar and on your member home page dashboard.  Email reminders can also be sent to you as the goals approach.

 

    

Customer Events

You can keep track of important events in the life of your Customer.  Some of these events may include birthdays, anniversaries, checkups, parties, reunions, etc.  The type of event is indicated by the icon to the left of the event date and name.  You can edit an event by clicking the pencil icon following the blue reminder date.  

The event can be specified as recurring monthly or annually and you can request to be automatically notified a specific number of days before the event.  If you've requested an email reminder, the date for the reminder will be displayed in blue after the event name.

Click the "Add New Event" link in the upper-right corner of the events list to add a new event.

 

  

  

Customer Mail

You can keep track of emails sent to the Customer in one place for future reference.  The email date, time and subject are displayed in the list.  You can click the preview icon to view the email.

 Click the "Send Mail" link in the upper-right corner of the mail list to send a new email message to the Customer.  

You will be able to optionally select from existing email templates created by the company, by yourself or by others.  You will learn more about creating email templates in the Custom Email Templates lesson in the User Tutorial.  If this lesson is not present in your User Tutorial you don't have access to this option. 

 

 

Customer Documents

Your Customer System provides the ability for you to attach documents or URLs to the Customer for future reference.  This is handy for saving Customer-related documents like deployment plans or agreements in one central place for easy review by others.  You can click the pencil icon to edit the document settings.  You can also click the preview icon to view the document or URL.

Click the "Add New Document" button to attach a new document or URL.

   

  

Export Customers

You can export Customers from your Customer System by clicking on the blue arrow icon to the right of the "Go" button.  A new window will display a list of all the Customers for the specified criteria in the Customer System search page.  You can copy and paste the displayed Customers into another program like Microsoft Excel.

  

   

 

You have learned how your Customer Management System helps you manage the ongoing relationships with your valued customers.

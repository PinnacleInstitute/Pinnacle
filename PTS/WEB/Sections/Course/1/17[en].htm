Your Lead Pages help you attract interested prospects to your offer.  Your Lead Pages are designed to be short and to the point in order to delivery your primary benefit to get their interest in just a few minutes.


Lead Pages

You may have several Lead Pages designed for specific purposes.  If you have multiple problems that you can solve, you should have a specific Lead Page for each benefit that you can deliver to your prospects.  Each Lead Page may contain text and pictures, Flash effects or audios and videos.  

To preview your Lead Page you can click the name of the Lead Page or you can click the 2nd Preview icon to the right of the Lead Page name.  This is just a preview and should not be sent to a prospect.  You can click the 1st icon to send the desired Lead Page to a friend.  The 3rd icon will give you instructions on how to promote your lead page and it will allow you to track your Lead Page's viewing statistics.

 

 

Email Lead Pages

Clicking the "Send to a Friend" icon will allow you to email the selected lead page to up to ten people at a time with the page below.  You can preview the selected Lead Page by clicking the "Preview" button.  You can also select a different Lead Page from the list. You can edit the subject line and you can personalize the body of the email message.  


 
Take care to not modify the special tags like {p-firstname}, which will be replaced with the person's first name when the email is sent.  Also make sure you don't make any changes to the link in the email message.  The link will contain specific information identifying this Lead Page, your member number and the prospect's number.

Your personal email signature is automatically inserted in the email message.  After you enter up to ten names and email addresses following the email message, you can also specify multiple keywords at the bottom of the page.  The keywords will be stored in each new prospect's description field for which you can search later.  This is handy for tracking the source of the new prospect, like a networking meeting or a seminar. 

You can use the WYSIWYG (What-You-See-Is-What-You-Get) Editor to apply formatting similar to a word processor.  You can use the two editor toolbars to select the font style, color and size, format and align the text, as well as many other functions.  

You can insert a new line immediately following the previous line by pressing the [shift]+[enter] keys together.  You can click the editor help icon near the top-right corner of the editor to learn more about how to use the WYSIWYG Editor.

Your new system strictly prohibits all spamming activities.  Spamming is the sending of any  unsolicited emails.  Besides being illegal, spamming is highly ineffective and it gives the false hope that you don't have to do anything.  Your most important job is to build relationships with people, don't attempt to have technology replace your relationship building.

When you click the "Add and Send" button at the bottom of the page, each new person receives a personal email from you, and they are added as a new prospect in your Sales Management System.

  

 

You can alternatively get to this email page by selecting the "Email Lead Page" menu option on the Sales Management System.

 

 

Lead Page Tracking

The sent Lead Page is linked to you.  When the prospect clicks the link in the email message you are immediately notified with the Quick Email Address that you learned about in the Member Profile lesson.  If you have your Quick Email Address set to your cell phone, you will receive a text message immediately.

The Lead Page is also personalized with your email signature.  Your email signature should contain your contact information for people who prefer to correspond with you directly by phone or email instead of submitting their information.

All Lead Pages include a capture form for the person to enter their name, email, phone and other pertinent information if they are interested. 

When the person enters their information and clicks the "Submit" button, you are again notified immediately that you have a new prospect.  The new prospect is presented with a thank you page and an optional link to start viewing the related sales presentation.

The person is automatically added as a new prospect in your Sales Management System.  If the prospect is already in your system, their existing prospect record will be updated with the newly submitted information.  The system tracks the dates and times when the prospect views the Lead Page and how many times they view it.  A prospect may be sent multiple Lead Pages over time if necessary.

Your responsibility is to start building a relationship with the new prospect as soon as they submit their information telling you they are interested in what you have to offer.  You should start qualifying the new prospect to find out how serious they are and provide additional information to progress them through the sales cycle.

 

 

Lead Page Usage

Clicking the 3rd "Usage" icon in your list of Lead Pages will help you learn how to use your Lead Page in online and offline advertising and promotions.

 

You personal Lead Page web address (URL) for the selected Lead Page is displayed on your usage page.  This is your URL to use for promoting this Lead Page.  You can review the reference articles on the bottom part of this page to learn more about how to promote your Lead Page to generate sales leads.

If you are using offline promotions like business cards, sizzle cards, classified adds, signs, posters, flyers, etc., you will want to register a domain name and have it forwarded to your Lead Page URL.

You can visit www.GoDaddy.com to register your own domain name.

If you are using online promotions like email signatures, forum signatures, social networking, pay-per-click advertising, newsletters, targeted website advertising, etc., you do not need to register a domain name because the user will not need to type the URL, they will just click on your link to go to your Lead Page.   

 

 

Lead Page Traffic Report

You can click the "Traffic Report" button on the usage page to view statistics on the number of people that viewed you lead page and submitted their information.

You can select a time frame from the list box, which automatically selects the from and to dates, or you can set the from and to dates explicitly.  Click the "Report" button to view the traffic statistics for the specified time period.

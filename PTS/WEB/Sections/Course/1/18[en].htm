Your Sales Presentations help you qualify a serious prospect after they've expressed an interest in your offer.  Your Sales Presentations are designed to deliver the details of your Sales Presentation in several pages to help your prospect internalize the benefits.


Sales Presentations

You may have several Sales Presentations designed for specific purposes.  If you have multiple solutions, you should have a specific Sales Presentation for each solution that you can provide to your prospects.  Each Sales Presentation may be a series of pages containing text and pictures, Flash effects or audios and videos.  


To preview your Sales Presentation you can click the name of the Sales Presentation or you can click the 2nd Preview icon to the right of the Sales Presentation name.  This is just a preview and should not be sent to a prospect.  You can click the 1st icon to send the desired Sales Presentation to a friend.  The last icon will display your personal Sales Presentation URL and it will allow you to track your Sales Presentation's viewing statistics.    

 

Personalize Your Presentations

You can define your personal messages to be inserted into the presentations.  This option may or may not appear depending on your personal options.  Click the Personalize option in the upper-right corner of your presentation list.  A page will be displayed allowing you to enter your personal welcome message, personal story, personal vision, personal testimony and personal invitation.

Click the pencil icon to edit your selected personal information.  These personal messages will be used on all presentations by default.  Each presentation in the list also includes a similar Personalize option to provide your personal messages for each specific presentation.  

The WYSIWYG editor supports the ability to insert pictures in your personal messages.  You must first upload your picture to the system.  Click the "Upload Images" option in the upper-right corner of your presentation list.  Click "Add New Document" in the upper right corner.   Enter a new document name for the image and click the "Upload File" button on the bottom of the page.  The page will refresh with the newly updated image file.  The full URL is displayed at the bottom of the page in purple.  Select the URL with your mouse and copy it (Control Key + C).  

Next in the WYSIWYG editor, place the cursor were you want the new image to be inserted.  Click the image icon on the right side of the 2nd toolbar.   Paste the image URL into the URL field by pressing the Control Key + V.  Your image will be displayed below.  You may also want to modify the width and height of your image,  and specify vertical and/or horizontal spacing around your image to separate your text from the image.  Finally, select the desired alignment option for your image (usually left or right).

When you click the Close button in your WYSIWYG editor, your personal messages page will be refreshed to display your recent changes.

 

 

Email Sales Presentations

You can email Sales Presentations to people that you've attracted personally and who are not in your Sales Management System as a prospect.  If they are already in the system from a Lead Page, you will send them a Sales Presentation from their detail prospect record.  You will learn how to do this later in the Sales Management lesson.

Clicking the "Send to a Friend" icon will allow you to email the selected Sales Presentation to up to ten people at a time with the page below.  You can preview the selected Sales Presentation by clicking the "Preview" button.  You can also select a different Sales Presentation from the list. You can edit the subject line and you can personalize the body of the email message.  


 
Take care to not modify the special tags like {p-firstname}, which will be replaced with the person's first name when the email is sent.  Also make sure you don't make any changes to the link in the email message.  The link will contain specific information identifying this Sales Presentation, your member number and the prospect's number.

Your personal email signature is automatically inserted in the email message.  After you enter up to ten names and email addresses following the email message, you can also specify multiple keywords at the bottom of the page.  The keywords will be stored in each new prospect's description field for which you can search later.  This is handy for tracking the source of the new prospect, like a networking meeting or a seminar. 

You can use the WYSIWYG (What-You-See-Is-What-You-Get) Editor to apply formatting similar to a word processor.  You can use the two editor toolbars to select the font style, color and size, format and align the text, as well as many other functions.  

You can insert a new line immediately following the previous line by pressing the [shift]+[enter] keys together.  You can click the editor help icon near the top-right corner of the editor to learn more about how to use the WYSIWYG Editor.

Your new system strictly prohibits all spamming activities.  Spamming is the sending of any  unsolicited emails.  Besides being illegal, spamming is highly ineffective and it gives the false hope that you don't have to do anything.  Your most important job is to build relationships with people, don't attempt to have technology replace your relationship building.

When you click the "Add and Send" button at the bottom of the page, each new person receives a personal email from you, and they are added as a new prospect in your Sales Management System.

  

You can alternatively get to this email page by selecting the "Presentations" menu option on the Sales Management System.

 

   

 

 

Sales Presentation Tracking

The sent Sales Presentation is linked to you.  When the prospect clicks the link in the email message you are immediately notified with the Quick Email Address that you learned about in the Member Profile lesson.  If you have your Quick Email Address set to your cell phone, you will receive a text message immediately.

Each page of the Sales Presentation is personalized with your email signature.  Your email signature should contain your contact information for the prospect to contact you directly by phone or email.

The system tracks the dates and times when the prospect views the Sales Presentation and how many times they view it.  The system also tracks which pages of the Sales Presentation the prospect views so you can tell what their interests are and how serious they are.  A prospect may be sent multiple Sales Presentations over time if necessary.

Your next step is to start closing your prospect after they recognize that they can benefit from what you have to offer.

   

  

Sales Presentation Usage

Clicking the 3rd "Usage" icon in your list of Sales Presentations will help you learn how to use your Sales Presentation web address (URL) directly outside of the sent email message.  This is not recommended because you won't have the tracking and notifications of the prospect's activities.

 

You personal Sales Presentation web address (URL) for the selected Sales Presentation is displayed on your usage page.  This is your URL to use for sharing this Sales Presentation outside of the email message.  

  

 

Sales Presentation Traffic Report

You can click the "Traffic Report" button on the usage page to view statistics on the number of people that viewed you Sales Presentation pages.

You can select a time frame from the list box, which automatically selects the from and to dates, or you can set the from and to dates explicitly.  Click the "Report" button to view the traffic statistics for the specified time period.

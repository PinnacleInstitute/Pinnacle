Your registered Training Classes help you track your training progress and monitor your test results.


My Classes

Your system includes a professional Learning Management System (LMS) that provides a university format of registered classes with multiple lessons and optional quizzes to facilitate an optimal learning experience.

  

The status of all your classes and lessons and the total amount of time spent and times viewed are tracked for your review and monitoring.  You can also view the length of each class, when you registered, and when you completed the class. 

Click the name of the class to view the lessons and start taking the training. 

 

You can also filter your list of classes by the status of the class.  You can view all classes or just classes that have not been started, or that have been started, or that have been completed, or that have passed or failed the training quizzes.

 

A training legend at the bottom of your class list indicates the status and options for your classes and lessons.  You will be able to easily see if a class or lesson has been started or if it has been completed, and if you have passed or failed the training quizzes.  You can also easily see if the course or lesson has video or audio and if it has a quiz. 

 

 

My Class Lessons

Your classes may contain multiple lessons with optional quizzes.  The lessons are listed with the same status and content indicators as the classes.  If you have taken a quiz for a lesson it will display the quiz score, the passing grade and the weighting of the quiz toward the final class grade.

For example, lesson number nine below has a quiz  with a passing grade of 70% and a weighting of 10% toward the final class grade.  The user passed the quiz with a score of 90%. 

Click the name of the lesson to start taking the training.

 

 

Start A Lesson

Your classes may contain video, audio, text and pictures, or any other training content that could appear on a web page.  A timer in the upper-right corner of the page keeps track of your time on the lesson.  In the upper-left corner you can take printable class notes during your lesson.   

 

The lesson status will be tracked and recorded if you stay on the lesson for at least 60 seconds or if you check the "Lesson Completed" checkbox at the bottom of the page.

You can click the "Next Lesson" button to automatically go to the next lesson.  You can also go to any lesson in the class by selecting a lesson from the list box.

If the lesson has a quiz you can take the quiz by clicking the "Take Quiz" button.  If you have already taken the quiz, you can review the quiz by clicking the "Review Quiz" button.

Clicking the "Return to My Class" button will take you back to your list of lessons for the current class.

 

 

Lesson Quiz

Your lesson quiz may have multiple questions with different score weightings on each question and it may have pictures, audio or video with its questions.

 

 

You can answer the questions and grade the quiz immediately by clicking the "Grade Quiz Now" button at the bottom of the page.  You can also retake the quiz by clicking the "Retake Quiz" button.  Not all quizzes may have the option to retake the quiz or to see the answers to the questions.

After you grade the quiz, your quiz grade and the passing grade are displayed at the top of the page. 

From the quiz you can go to the next lesson, go to any other lesson, return to the same lesson, or return to the class listing of all the lessons.  

 

 

Course Evaluation

After you complete all the lessons for a class, you will be prompted to evaluate the course for feedback to the course trainer and for feedback for other students.  You can review your evaluation at any time from the class page.

 

 

Class Certificate

After you complete all the lessons for a class, you will be able to print a professional certificate suitable for framing by clicking the "Print Certificate" button on the bottom of the class page.  

If you have a passing grade you will be able to print a "Certificate of Achievement".  If the class did not require a passing grade or you did not achieve a passing grade you will be able to print a "Certificate of Completion".

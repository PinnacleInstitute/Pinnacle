Course Catalog

Your professional Learning Management System (LMS) includes a library of training and education courses to help you improve.  Your course catalog provides a powerful Google-like search engine to help you find exactly what you are looking for.  Click the question mark icon to the right of the "Search" button to learn more about the advanced searching capabilities.  You can search for keywords in the course name, description or instructor's name.


The search results are listed twenty courses at a time. on the right side of the result list you can click the "Next Page" and "Previous Page" links to view the next or previous twenty courses.  If the list is too long, you may want to be more specific in your search criteria.

You can also browse categories of courses by clicking the yellow folders below.  Some folders may also include subfolders of courses.  Clicking the plus   and minus   icons will expand and collapse the folder structure.  Clicking the folders or folder names will display a list of courses below.

You can hide the course folders by clicking the Folder Close icon .  You can redisplay the course folders by clicking the Folder Open icon . 

If you know a specific course number, you can entering it in the "Course #" textbox and click the "Go" button.  Some related courses are combined into a series of courses.  If you know a specific series number, you can entering it in the "Series #" textbox and click the "Go" button. 

 

 

Selected Courses

A list of the requested courses will be displayed on the bottom half of the page when you click the "Search" button, "Go" button, or if you click on a yellow folder .

The course list displays the course name, description, course number, instructor, and course length.  Other icons indicate if the course has audio , video or a quiz . 

Your course catalog includes a course rating system to help you evaluate the quality of a course before you register for it.  Every student that takes a course is prompted upon completion to evaluate the course.  The system automatically calculates a 1 - 5 star ranking for each course and displays the result to the left of each course name.  

Only courses evaluated within the last 90 days are used in the star ranking.  You can hover your mouse over the star ranking of each class and a small popup window will display how many classes were rated.  You can also click the star ranking to view the detail evaluations and the students comments.

When you are ready to register for a course, click the Course Register icon following the course name.  You will be presented with a confirmation and an option to return to the course list.  Your newly registered course will appear in your list of registered classes.

  

 

Course Ranking

Click the star ranking to the left of the course name to view the detail evaluations and the students comments for the selected course.  You can view the detail ratings on the instructor, the content, the presentation and the usefulness.  You can also view the detail comments from students that left comments.  You can see when the course was completed and how soon they said they would apply what they learned and to whom they would recommend the course.

 

You can view a subset of the evaluations by selecting the buttons at the bottom of the page.  By default the list shows all completed classes.  You can also view only classes that have been evaluated or only classes that left comments.

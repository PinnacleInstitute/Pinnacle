Group Options

Members can access your group resources by entering your group number on their "My Info" page in the group # field.  Your group number is the same as your member number.

You can customize the graphic header that is displayed at the top of the page when a member of your group accesses the system. Click the "Upload Header" button to select a graphic file from your personal computer to be used for your custom group header.  The graphic header file should be 750 pixels wide with a height typically from 60 to 120 pixels.

You can also create a custom web page that will be displayed in the middle of the member home page just after the dashboard.  You can put any content on this page that you would like.  Typically you would include links to custom information pages with content specific to your group.  Click the "Update" button when you are finished editing your custom member page.

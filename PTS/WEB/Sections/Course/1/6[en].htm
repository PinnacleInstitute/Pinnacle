Your Custom Signatures allow you to create multiple personal signatures for specific use within lead pages, presentations, contact emails, prospect emails and customer emails.  These signatures are inserted by default in their respective uses and can be edited with each use.



To add more custom signatures you can click the "More Signatures..." link in the top-right corner above the WYSIWYG Editor. 

 

 

 

More Signatures

This will display a list of all your custom signatures.  To add a new signature click the "Add New Signature" link in the upper-right corner of the page.

 

 

    

  

Add New Signature

 A custom signature may be for any specific lead page or all lead pages, any specific presentation or all presentations, all contact emails, all prospect emails, or all customer emails.

First select the type of signature.  If it is for a specific lead page or presentation then enter the unique number of the lead page or presentation.  The unique number is displayed in the lead page and presentation lists in purple following the name.  If it is for all lead pages or all presentations, leave the number set to 0.  The contact, prospect and customer emails do not use the number. 

 

You can edit your custom signature in the WYSIWYG (What-You-See-Is-What-You-Get) Editor to apply formatting similar to a word processor.  You can use the three editor toolbars to select the font style, color and size, format and align the text, insert your image in your signature, as well as many other functions.  

To insert your image, select the picture icon near the end of the second toolbar.  In the popup window, click the "Member" folder icon.  Scroll down the file list searching for the image file with your member number as the filename. When you select a filename it will be displayed on the left side of the popup window.  If this is the correct image, click the "OK" button to return to the editor.

You may want to center align your newly inserted image with your contact information below it by selecting everything with the mouse and clicking the small center-align icon near the middle of the second toolbar. 

You can insert a new line immediately following the previous line by pressing the [shift]+[enter] keys together.  

 

 

Signature Uses

 There are 6 different type of signatures and 10 different places that your signature is used.

1. Default Signature
Used anywhere a more specific signature is not defined.
 
2. Lead Page Signature
Lead Page
Lead Page thank you
 
3. Presentation Signature 
Presentation pages
 
4. Contact Email Signature
Email a Contact  (from the Contact Manager)
Email New Prospects  (from the Sales System)
Email Lead Page  (from Prospect detail page)
 
5. Prospect Email Signature
Email a Presentation to a Prospect  (from the Prospect detail page)
Email a Prospect  (from the Prospect detail page)
Email generated from a status change  (optional from the Prospect detail page)
 
6. Customer Email Signature
Email a Customer  (from the Customer Service System)
 

  

  

Signature Precedence

The Lead Pages and Presentations first look for a signature specific to themselves.  

If not found, they look for a general signature for any lead page or presentation respectively.  

If not found, they look for a default signature.  

If not found, they use the standard email signature on the My Info page.

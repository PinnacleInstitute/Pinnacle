Quick Access Shortcuts

 

 

Similar to most graphical operating systems like Microsoft Windows, you can create shortcuts to important and frequently accessed information.  Your Quick Access Shortcuts are displayed near the top of your member home page just below your quick access icons.  Your new system can save and display shortcuts icons on your member home page to contacts, prospects, customers, affiliates, goals, classes, assessments, newsletters, documents, emails, tasks, and projects.  You may or may not have access to all these options.

 

 

 

Create Shortcuts

You can create a shortcut to important or frequently accessed information by going to the detail page of the desired item.  For example;  The Contact detail page displays a shortcut icon at the top of the page following the contact's name.

 

 

 

 

Click the shortcut icon to create a shortcut to the contact.  A popup window will confirm that the shortcut was created successfully.  Click the "Close" button to close the popup window.

 

 

Click the "Home" menu option on the top-left side of the page

to view your selected Quick Access Shortcuts near the top of your member home page.  Newly created shortcuts are pinned to the member home page by default.

 

 

 

 You can edit your Quick Access Shortcuts by  clicking the "Shortcuts" menu option on the top-left side of the page.

 

 

All of your selected shortcuts are displayed and are organized in the list by type.  The red pin icon indicates that the shortcut will appear at the top of the list and it will appear near the top of your member home page.  Click the name of the shortcut to go directly to the selected item.

Click the Unpin icon to remove the selected shortcut from the top of the list.   Click the Pin icon to move the selected icon to the top part of the list and display it on your member home page.  Click the Edit icon to change the shortcut name and pin status. Click the trash can icon to remove the selected shortcut completely from the list. 

  

 

  

 

You have learned how your Quick Access Icons, at the top of your member home page, provide easy access to your important and frequent data.

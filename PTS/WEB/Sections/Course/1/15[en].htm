Your Assessments and Certifications assist you with determining your strengths and weaknesses and recognizing your competencies. 


My Assessments

All your Assessments and Certifications that you have already taken are listed for your review.  They may be available for several different purposes.  Assessments may be for testing your specific competencies, or for diagnosing problems with decision-tree logic based on responses to questions, or they may be introspective assessments to help you internally examine yourself. 

You can click on the preview icon to view the assessment again.  Click the report icon to view the results of the assessment.  Click the trash can icon to remove the selected assessment from your list.  The removed assessment is not permanently deleted and can be restored by the administrative support. 

Certifications are also listed with your assessments.  The certification icon is displayed at the beginning of the certification name.  If you have completed the certification with a passing grade, a Passed Certification icon will be displayed.  If you did not pass the certification, a Failed Certification icon will be displayed.  Certifications may have a time limit specified.

If you passed the certification, a certificate icon will be displayed on the right side of the list.  Click the certificate icon to print a professional certificate suitable for framing.  

Assessments and Certifications may have a limit on the number of times that you can take them.  They may also have a required number days that you must wait before taking it again.  Each Assessment report may include a list of recommended courses related to the assessment that you can register for.  

You can take a new assessment by clicking the "Take New Assessment" link in the upper-right corner of the page.

  

 

Assessment

Each assessment may consist of one or more pages of questions and each page may have one or more questions.  The questions may be multiple choice with optional multiple selections, true/false, fill-in-the-blank, prioritized answers, ranked answers, or informational text boxes for qualitative feedback.   

 

 

Assessment Report

You can click the report icon in your Assessment list to view the results of the assessment.  Some assessments may have a calculated score.  The results of your Assessment and the optional score are displayed on your Assessment Report.

Your Assessment Report may also display a list of recommended courses related to the assessment.

All of the assessment questions and your answers are displayed on the Assessment Report for your review later.  


 

 

Take New Assessment

Clicking the "Take New Assessment" link in the upper-right corner of your Assessments page will display a list of the available assessments for you to take.

Click the "Take This Assessment" icon to take the selected assessment.


    

 

Certification Certificate

After you complete the certification with a passing grade you will be able to print a professional certificate suitable for framing by clicking the Certificate icon on the right side of your Assessments list.

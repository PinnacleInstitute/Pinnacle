You can subscribe to multiple News Letters with valuable information to help you with your business.


   

My Newsletters

A list of your Newsletter subscriptions is displayed for your review.  A list of featured newsletters is also displayed following your subscription list.  The newletter name and desciption are displayed along with the newsletter # and the editor's name in purple.

You can view the newsletter archives by clicking the preview  icon on the right side of the list.

You can cancel your subscription by clicking the trash can  icon on the right-side of your subscription list.

You can start a new subscription by clicking the add  icon on the right-side of the featured newsletter list.  

You can also view a list of all available newsletters by clicking the "View More Newsletters" option in the upper-right corner above the featured newsletters list.

Click the refresh  icon at the top of the page after subscribing to a new newsletter.





 

  

View More Newsletters

You can search all available newsletters by any word in the newletter name or description, or search by the editor's member #.   

You can view the newsletter archives by clicking the preview  icon on the right side of the list.

You can start a new subscription by clicking the add  icon on the right-side of the featured newsletter list.  

 

 

   

 

  

Newsletter Subscriptions

Your subscribed newsletters will be automatically emailed to you whenever a new issue is published by the editor.

 

 

 

  

Prospect Subscriptions

You can create a new subscription for one of your prospects that has given you their permission to receive the newsletter.

Enter the newsletter # on the prospect detail page at the bottom of the Description folder tab.  The newsletter # is displayed following the newsletter name in your list of available newsletters.  Set the prospect's newsletter status to Active for the prospect to start receiving the newsletter.

 

 

  

Unsubscribe from a Newsletter

Every newsletter that is emailed to a member or a prospect has an option at the bottom of the email to unsubscribe.

Your Dynamic Calendar can display your appointments and date-time sensitive information from the system for your review of accomplishments and upcoming activities.



Dynamic Calendar

Your Dynamic Calendar allows you to manage your appointments and all your other events in the system.  Your Dynamic Calendar reaches into the system and pulls out all date-time sensitive information and automatically displays it for your review and action.  

 


 

Click the name of any item on your calendar to view a popup window of detail information.  Items on your calendar may be explicitly set appointments, due goals, contact callbacks, prospect next steps, prospect sales notes, customer follow-up events, classes, assessments, due projects or due project tasks.

You can view different months and years on your Calendar by clicking the arrows at the top of the Calendar surrounding the month and year.  Click the previous or arrows to view the previous year or month.  Click the next or arrows to view the next month or year.

Click the icons in the upper-right corner of your calendar to add a new appointment, refresh your calendar, edit your calendar options or create a new calendar.  Details for this functionality is described below.

Daily Calendar

Click the underlined day number on your monthly calendar to view all events for the selected day.  Click the name of any item on your daily calendar to view a popup window of detail information.  If you change any information in the detail popup window, click the "Refresh" button at the bottom of the daily calendar to view the changes.

 

 


  

 

Add New Appointment

Click the Add New Appointment icon in the upper-right corner of your monthly calendar to add a new appointment.  You can also make a copy of an existing appointment by clicking the name of the appointment on your calendar and in the popup window, click the the "Duplicate" button.

Enter the name of the appointment in the subject textbox.  Enter an optional appointment location.  The start and end date will default to today and the appointment will be set to all day.  Enter the time for the appointment (e.g. 10:00am) and uncheck the "All Day Appointment" checkbox.  An appointment that spans multiple days is displayed in purple on all days that it appears on the calendar. 

Select the Public Appointment checkbox to specify an appointment as a public appointment if you want others to take a time slot that you have designated as available.  This is useful for allowing others to sign up for available time slots for training, support, customer service, etc.  You will learn more about public appointments later in this lesson.     

You can select the type of appointment and set it's importance and how you want the appointment to be shown on your public calendar when viewed by others. A Private appointment will not be displayed on your public calendar. A Busy appointment will be displayed without any details shown.  

You can request an appointment reminder to be emailed to you by selecting an option from the Reminder list box.  This will automatically populate the reminder date and time text box when you add the appointment.  If you leave the Reminder list box unselected, you can enter your own custom date and time to be reminded.

You can enter an optional description of up to 500 characters.

You can specify this new appointment as a weekly, monthly or annually recurring appointment by selecting the appropriate option in the Recurring textbox.  The appointment will recur forever unless you specify an end date in the Recurring through textbox.

Select the "Meeting Planner" checkbox if you would like to use the advanced functionality to plan a meeting with invitations, guest lists and RSVP tracking.  You will learn more about this capability in the Meeting Planner lesson in the User Tutorial.  If you do not have this lesson available, then you do not have the Meeting Planner option enabled. 

Click the Add button when you are ready to create your new appointment.  The page will be refreshed with your new appointment added to your calendar.  If you had previously selected the "Meeting Planner" checkbox, a "Plan Meeting" button will appear at the bottom of the page. 

Click the "Close" button to close the appointment window and click the Refresh Calendar icon to view the new appointment on your calendar.

     

 

Public Appointments

You can specify an appointment as a public appointment if you want others to take a time slot that you have designated as available.  This is useful for allowing others to sign up for available time slots for training, support, customer service, etc.  

Give the public access URL displayed at the bottom of your calendar to anyone that you want to be able to sign up for appointments on your calendar.  When they access your calendar they will see the available time slots.  When they click on the appointment name a popup window will display all the appointment details and prompt the user for a security code.  Your member # is the security code that you must provide to them.  They will also be prompted to enter their name and subject matter in the subject textbox.

When they click the "Update" button with a valid security code entered, the appointment will be saved and the appointment will no longer be available as a public appointment.  You will also receive an email from the system notifying you of the taken time slot.

If you need to reset a public appointment, click the appointment name on your calendar to reenter the subject name and reselect the Public Appointment checkbox. 

  

  

Refresh Calendar

Click the Refresh Calendar icon in the upper-right corner of your monthly calendar to display any new appointments or new changes you may have made to any of the items on your calendar.

 

 

Edit Calendar Options

Click the Edit Calendar Options icon in the upper-right corner of your monthly calendar to edit the configuration options for the current calendar.

You can edit the name of your calendar and the order it will appear in the calendar list box in the upper-right corner of your calendar.  Select the time zone as a reference for others who may be looking at your calendar.  Select the "Private Calendar" checkbox if you don't want your calendar to be publicly accessible.  You can enter a short description for your own reference.

Select the appropriate checkboxes for the items to be displayed on your calendar.

Click the "Update" button to save your changes.  You can also click the "Delete" button to remove this calendar completely from the system.

 

 

Create New Calendar

You can have multiple calendars for various purposes, including a personal appointment calendar, a group leadership calendar, a sales activity calendar, etc.  Each calendar may have its own options of what is displayed on the calendar.  Your calendars are listed in the list box in the upper-right corner of your calendar.  

Click the Create New Calendar icon in the upper-right corner of your monthly calendar to create another calendar with its own set of options.

Group Members

 

You can view the list of group members that have access to your group resources.  Members can access your group resources by entering your group number on their "My Info" page in the group # field.  Your group number is the same as your member number.

The icon to the left of the member's name designates their status as premium , trial , standard , or inactive .  

Click the preview icon to view a popup window displaying the member's contact information.

You can remove a member from your group by clicking the blue trash can icon on the right side of the list.  The star icon identifies you as the group administrator.

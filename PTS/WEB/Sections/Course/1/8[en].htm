You can Mentor Others as a qualified leader by taking them by the virtual hand and showing them how to be successful.


 

Mentoring

You can be an invaluable resource to those you mentor.  Your system may assign mentors or it may allow members to select their own personal mentor.  If your options allow for selecting a mentor you will see a "My Mentor's #" text box in the upper right corner of your mentoring page.  The first thing you want your new person to do is to specify you as their mentor.  Give them your mentoring number that is displayed near the top of your mentoring page following your name.

Have them enter your mentoring number in the upper-right corner of their mentoring page and click the "Update" button.  The page will be refreshed and you will be displayed as their new mentor.  The system will also send you an email to let you know that they've selected you as their mentor.  They can view your contact information by clicking the preview icon following your name.

They may have multiple mentors over time, but only one at a time.  They can enter the new mentor's number and click the "Update" button to change their mentor.  

 

 

Those You Mentor

A list of all those you mentor is displayed with optional pictures.  They can learn about uploading their personal pictures in the Member Profile lesson.  You can view their contact information by clicking the preview icon following their name.  The date and time that they last accessed the system is displayed on the right side of the list.

 

Click the icons below their name to view their performance summary, those they mentor, their calendars, goals, contacts, prospects, customers, assessments, courses, notes and your notification requests.

Click the Notifications icon to request to be automatically notified when they have an accomplishment including when they access the system, complete a class, complete an assessment, complete a certification, complete a goal or close a sale.  The notification icon will change to a notifications "On" icon if you have requested any notifications for the selected person.

If you chose to not mentor someone for any reason you can remove them from your mentoring list by clicking the trash can icon on the right side of the list.

   

 

Mentoring Notes

Click the Notes icon to view the selected person's notes and add a new note.  If the person has a new note the Notes icon will be blinking.  Your Member Home Page will also display a red flashing mentoring note notice on your dashboard.   

After reviewing all the new mentoring notes for those you mentor, you can click the "Mark All Notes As Read" button at the bottom of the page.  This will remove all red note indicators and all blinking note icons . 

 Your mentoring notes are listed in chronological order with the most recent notes at the top.  You can edit notes that you have posted by clicking the pencil icon at the end of the note.  To add a new mentoring note, click the  "Add New Note" icon in the upper-right corner of the Notes list.

   

 

Add New Note

You can enter up to 1000 characters in each new note.  The current date and time are automatically set in the date field above the note and can be changed if necessary. If you want the note to always stay at the top of the list, you can select the checkbox called "Pin to the top of the list" found below the Notes text box.  

 

 

 

Printable Notes

You can view a complete list of printable mentoring notes by clicking the "Printable Notes" link in the upper-right corner of the Notes list.

 

 

Email Mentoring Team

From your Mentoring page you can send one email message to your entire mentoring team.  Click the email icon to popup your email program with all the email addresses in the To: line.  You can Copy & Paste the email addresses to a different email program if needed. 

 

 

View Mentoring Teams

From your Mentoring page you can click the mentoring team icon to view your mentoring teams for up to four levels of mentors.  Click any person's name to view up to four more levels below them.  The purple dates and times are when they enrolled and when they last visited the system.  You can view their contact information by clicking the preview icon at the end of the line.

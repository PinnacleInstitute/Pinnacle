Sales Reports

Your Sales Management System includes several Sales Reports to help you monitor your sales  success by measuring how effectively you are filling your sales pipeline, moving your prospects through your sales pipeline, and moving your prospects out of your sales pipeline by disqualifying them or converting them into customers.

The Sales System menu in the upper-right corner of the page includes a Reports section to access your Sales Reports.

 

 

Sales Board

A traditional sales department had a large white board or chalk board mounted on the wall that displayed all the active deals the salespeople were working on and what the status of each deal was.  It provided a simple summary for management to monitor the status of the sales department.

Your personal Sales Board provides a summary of all your active prospects for the selected Sales Campaign at the top of the page.  Your Sales Board is sometimes referred to as your sales pipeline.  

Your Sales Board displays all the sales statuses for the selected sales campaign with the total prospects per status and their potential and current value.  The current value is calculated from the entered potential value for each prospect and their current status in the sales cycle.  

The Sales Campaign defines a conversion rate for each sales status.  For example, the "Retail" sales campaign below has three statuses with conversion rates of 20%, 50% and 90%.  It assumes that on average you will close 2 out of 10 prospects that make it to the Qualify status, 5 out of 10 that make it to the Evaluate status, and 9 out of 10 that make it to the Commit status. 

Select one of the Sales Campaigns from the list box to display the Sales Board results.  

All prospects for each step of the sales campaign are listed along with their agreed upon next step, date, time and potential value.  Click the pencil icon to edit the prospect details or click the note icon to edit the prospect's notes.

 

 

Sales Schedule

Your Sales Schedule lists all prospects and their next step, date, time and status that have an agreed upon next step scheduled within the date range selected at the top of the page.  

You can select an option from the "Report On" list box to populate the date range or you can set the From and To dates explicitly,  Click the calendar icons to view a popup calendar.

Click the "Report" button to display the Sales Schedule results.

 

Click the pencil icon to edit the prospect details or click the note icon to edit the prospect's notes.

 

  

Sales Activity Report

Your Sales Activity Report lists all prospect notes with dates that are within the date range listed at the top of the page.  

You can select an option from the "Report On" list box to populate the date range or you can set the From and To dates explicitly,  Click the calendar icons to view a popup calendar.

You can view your sales activities for the selected date range using one of three different methods.  Click the "List All Activity Notes" button to view all notes that are not reminders.  Click the "List All Reminder Notes" button to view all notes that are reminders.  You can alternately search the contents of the notes for a specific keyword either starting at the beginning of the note or contained anywhere in the note.

Click the pencil icon to edit the prospect details or click the note icon to edit the specific note.

 

  

First Step Report

Your First Step Report lists all prospects that moved to the first step of the sales campaign within the date range listed at the top of the page.  This is an important metric to measure how well you are filling your sales pipeline.

You can select an option from the "Report On" list box to populate the date range or you can set the From and To dates explicitly,  Click the calendar icons to view a popup calendar.

Click the "Report" button to display the First Step Report results.

Click the pencil icon to edit the prospect details or click the note icon to edit the prospect's notes.

  

 

Closed Sales Report

Your Closed Sales Report lists all prospects that closed within the date range listed at the top of the page.

You can select an option from the "Report On" list box to populate the date range or you can set the From and To dates explicitly,  Click the calendar icons to view a popup calendar.

Click the "Report" button to display the Closed Sales Report results.

Click the pencil icon to edit the prospect details or click the note icon to edit the prospect's notes.

 

 

Fallback Report

Your Fallback Report lists all prospects that are in the Fallback status.  These are the prospects that are unwilling or unable to commit to a next step.  You will want to create strategies for reactivating these prospects.

Click the pencil icon to edit the prospect details or click the note icon to edit the prospect's notes.

 

  

Distributed Leads Report

Your Distributed Leads Report lists a summary of how many leads you distributed to which members during the selected date range.  

You can select an option from the "Report On" list box to populate the date range or you can set the From and To dates explicitly,  Click the calendar icons to view a popup calendar.

Click the "Report" button to display the Distributed Leads Report results.

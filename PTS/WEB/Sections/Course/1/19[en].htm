Your Sales Management System helps you track the progress of your prospects through your sales cycle.


Sales Management

Your system includes a professional Sales Management System similar to corporate sales systems like SalesForce.com.

 

Prospects are those who have expressed an interest in your offer.  If they have not yet expressed an interest then they are just suspects and can be best managed in your Contact Manager.  After you close a prospect you will be able to further manage the ongoing customer relationship in your Customer Management System.  You can learn more about the Contact Manager and the Customer Management System in the corresponding lessons in your User Tutorial.  If these lessons are not in your User Tutorial, then you do not have these options available.      

 

  

Sales System Menus

Your Sales System provides several menus in the upper-right corner of the page for accessing additional features of your system.  

The "Leads" menu section enables you to email a Lead Page to a new prospect, preview your Lead Pages, distribute Leads to others, and go to your Contact Manager. 

 

The "Prospects" menu section enables you to email a Sales Presentation to a new prospect, preview your Sales Presentations, directly add a new prospect, manage your affiliate partners, and go to your Customer Management System.  Some of these options may not be available depending on your access options. 

 

The "Reports" menu section allows you to view several Sales Reports that you will learn more about in the Sales Report lesson in your User Tutorial.

 

 

Search Prospects 

 

You can search for an existing prospect by any of the fields in the "Search By" list box.  Enter the text you want to search for in the "Find" text box and click the "Go" button.  The search text does not need to be an exact match.  The system will search for all prospects that start with the entered search text in the selected field.  If you search by the Prospect Name or Description, the system will list all prospects where the search text is anywhere within the Name or Description field respectively. 

The search results are also sorted by the selected search field.  If you enter nothing in the search text box, all prospects will be listed sorted by the search field.  If you select the Entry Date search field with no search text, the prospects will be listed with the most current dates at the top of the list.  This is a handy way of listing your newest prospects. 

You can further filter your list of prospects by specifying a status in the second list box. New prospects have no status yet, Active prospects have any status except New, Fallback, Closed or Dead, Live prospects have any status except Closed or Dead, and the Fallback, Closed and Dead statuses are described in the Prospect Sales Status section below.

The selected prospects are listed twenty prospects at a time.  On the right side of the result list you can click the "Next Page" and "Previous Page" links to view the next or previous twenty prospects.  If the list is too long, you may want to be more specific in your search criteria.

When the system returns a list of selected prospects, click the name of the prospect to view and edit the detailed contact information.

 

  

Distribute Leads

You can share your prospect leads with others.  This is handy for redistributing leads to other members of your team to help out new people or for geographic purposes or for redistributing leads acquired from a co-operative advertising campaign with others.  

You can distribute leads to others by selecting the "Distribute Leads" menu option in the "Leads" menu section in the upper-right corner of your Sales System.

 

You will be prompted to enter the member # of the person to receive the leads.  You can also choose to distribute leads captured from a specific lead page or from all lead pages.  Click the "Go" button after making your selections.

A list will be displayed of all your prospects acquired from the specified lead page option.  Prospects are excluded that have the "Do Not Distribute" checkbox selected in the prospect's contact folder tab.

Click the checkboxes on the left-side of the page for all prospects that you want to distribute to the selected member displayed at the top of the page.

You can also click the buttons at the bottom of the page to check and uncheck all prospects or ten at a time.

Click the "Distribute Checked Leads" button when you are ready to distribute all prospects with a checked checkbox to the selected member.  The selected prospect leads will be moved to the selected member.  The distributed prospect leads will be designated at the bottom of the Contact Folder with who sent the lead and when they sent it.

The member receiving the distributed prospect leads will be sent an email from the sender listing all the newly distributed leads.

  

 

Adding New Prospects

There are several ways to add a new prospect into your Sales System.  

The first option is from your Contact Manager.  As you are working your list of contacts, when you find an interested contact, you can move them into your Sales System as a new contact by clicking the "Move To Prospect" button on the bottom of the contact detail page.  You can learn more about your Contact Manager in the corresponding lesson in your User Tutorial.

You can also add a new prospect into your Sales System by emailing them a Lead Page to get their interest, or by emailing them a Sales Presentation if you already have their interest.  These two options are available from the Sales System menu in the upper-right corner of the page in the "Lead" and "Prospect" menu sections.  You can learn more about your Lead Pages and Sales Presentations in the corresponding lessons in your User Tutorial.

You can also add a new prospect directly into the system by selecting the "Add New Prospect" menu option from the Sales System menu in the upper-right corner of the page in the "Prospect" menu section.

 

 

Edit Prospect

A summary of the prospect information is displayed near the top of the page in the light blue bar.  It includes the prospect's number, sales campaign, prospect type and the date they were added to the system.

 

You can edit the prospect's name, type, potential value, category and their priority.  The potential value is used by the sales reports and the priority is an easy way for you to organize your hot prospects.  The priority textbox can contain up to four letters or numbers to support any prioritization system that best meets your needs.  For example; you could use A, B or C;  OR  1, 2 or 3;  OR  1a, 1b, 1c, 2a, 2b, 2c, 3a, 3b or 3c; etc.

You can also select the specific Sales Campaign for this prospect.  Click the "Change" button following the Sales Campaign to make a new selection.  The Sales Campaign defines the steps in the sales cycle.  These steps are displayed and tracked in the sales status section below.

You can also preview the lead page and sales presentation that was sent to the prospect.  You can see how many times they viewed the lead page or sales presentation, and if they replied to the lead page and which pages of the sales presentation they viewed.  You can click the presentation "Pages" link to view a list of the pages they viewed and did not view.

You can click the "Send" button to email a lead page or sales presentation to the prospect.  If a second lead page or sales presentation is emailed to the prospect, the original one is logged in the prospect's notes by the system.

When you are done reviewing or changing the prospect's information you can click the "Update" button to save your changes and stay on the detail prospect page, or click the "Update & Exit" button to save your changes and return to the Sales System, or click the "Return" button to return to the Sales System without saving your changes.  

Additional prospect information is organized in several folder tabs as described below.    

 

  

Prospect Sales Status

The previously selected Sales Campaign defines the steps in the sales cycle.  You can keep track of which step they are on and the dates that they reached each step.  Select an option from the Sales Status list box to specify which step they are on.  You can enter the corresponding milestone date below or if you leave it blank the system will fill it in for you with the current date when you update the prospect information.

The "Agreed Upon Next Step" helps you track what the next step is with the prospect.  The Next Step date and time will automatically appear on your calendar and on your member home page dashboard.  If the prospect will not agree to a next step, then are probably not an active prospect and should be placed in the Fallback status.

The Sales System automatically includes several statuses for every prospect.  The "New" status is when the prospect is first added to the system.  The Fallback, Closed and Dead statuses are for when the prospect will not agree to a next step, when the sales is consummated, or when the sales opportunity is completed lost respectively.

The statuses between the New and Fallback steps are defined by the selected Sales Campaign.  In the example above, the Qualify, Evaluate and Commit steps are defined by the prospect's selected Sales Campaign.

Some Sales Steps predefined by a Sales Campaign may include advanced features like automatically sending an email to the prospect or the salesperson and automatically changing to a specified status in a specified period of time.  These features support automated sales activities like auto-responders and archived newsletter distribution. 

  

 

Prospect Contact Information

You can keep track of the prospect's contact information including the primary contact and their title, email and phone numbers, as well as the prospect's address and website.  Additional contact information can be tracked in the prospect's description or notes.

You can also indicate that the prospect is not to be redistributed by the Lead Distribution feature that was described above.

  

 

  

Prospect Description

The prospect's description can contain up to 2000 characters to track any additional information.  You can also enter unlimited additional information in the prospect's notes.  

If you know that the prospect's status is going to change in the near future, you can tell the system to automatically make the status change for you on a specific date.  An optional automated sales step may also populate the auto status change feature.

 

 

 

Prospect Notes

You can enter an unlimited number of prospect notes for up to 1000 characters each.  The system will display the date, time and author of the note.  You can edit any notes you added by clicking the pencil icon following the note.  You cannot edit notes posted by others or by the system.  All notes are listed in chronological order except for notes that are explicitly pinned to the top of the list.  This is handy for reference information.   

The Sales System will automatically add notes to log prospect activities like when they were emailed a lead page or sales presentation, when they started viewing a lead page or presentation, when a lead page or sales presentation was replaced, etc.    

Click the "Add New Note" link in the upper-right corner of the notes list to add a new note.

 

You can add a note as a reminder of something to do later for this prospect.  When adding or editing a reminder note, set the date to the desired future date and select the Reminder checkbox on the bottom of the page.  The reminder note will appear on your calendar and home page dashboard.

   

 

Prospect Events

You can keep track of important events in the life of your prospect.  Some of these events may include birthdays, anniversaries, checkups, parties, reunions, etc.  The type of event is indicated by the icon to the left of the event date and name.  You can edit an event by clicking the pencil icon following the blue reminder date.  

The event can be specified as recurring monthly or annually and you can request to be automatically notified a specific number of days before the event.  If you've requested an email reminder, the date for the reminder will be displayed in blue after the event name.

Click the "Add New Event" link in the upper-right corner of the events list to add a new event.

 

  

  

Prospect Mail

You can keep track of emails sent to the prospect in one place for future reference.  The email date, time and subject are displayed in the list.  You can click the preview icon to view the email.

 Click the "Send Mail" link in the upper-right corner of the mail list to send a new email message to the prospect.  

You will be able to optionally select from existing email templates created by the company, by yourself or by others.  You will learn more about creating email templates in the Custom Email Templates lesson in the User Tutorial.  If this lesson is not present in your User Tutorial you don't have access to this option. 

 

 

Prospect Documents

Your Sales System provides the ability for you to attach documents or URLs to the prospect for future reference.  This is handy for saving prospect-related documents like proposals or agreements in one central place for easy review by others.  You can click the pencil icon to edit the document settings.  You can also click the preview icon to view the document or URL.

Click the "Add New Document" button to attach a new document or URL.

  

 

Export Prospects

You can export prospects from your Sales System by clicking on the blue arrow icon to the right of the "Go" button.  A new window will display a list of all the prospects for the specified criteria in the Sales System search page.  You can copy and paste the displayed prospects into another program like Microsoft Excel.

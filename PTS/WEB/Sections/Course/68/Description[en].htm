They lost a loved one.  They almost lost hope.

They searched for answers about the purpose of life as they struggled with the trials and temptations of today's society. 

Then the influence of a friend set them on an unexpected course that promised the understanding and hapiness they sought - through living the timeless teachings of Jesus Christ.

On The Way Home is a moving story that speaks to each of us - of lasting hapiness, closer relationships, and true inner peace.

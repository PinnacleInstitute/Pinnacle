Course Requirements

You will know whether you meet the course requirements, that you be on the path to spiritual awakening!  If you feel a surge of recognition and truth, then follow this and do the course.

Course Goals

The goals of the course are that you awaken to your full potential of mind body emotions and spirit.  Your own personal goals on the path will form part of the goals of this course.  At another level, you will learn what Mastery means, and how Mastery can literally change your life.

Course Materials

The course is self-contained, although you will be given further reading at each stage, designed to assist your understanding of the subject matter.

Grading Policy

You will also know when you have achieved Mastery at each of the levels.  Nevertheless, you can submit essays on each topic for assessment.  These will be graded pass/retry only.  You can no more fail at Mastery than you can fail to live!

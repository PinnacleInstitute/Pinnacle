Story Six

Fortunately police officers arrived at the scene in time prevent the fracas from escalating

from the inchoate stage.

      "It's still in the inchoate stage," John, a reporter for the local radio station, said.

      "What happened?" the news anchor asked.

     "It was merely a malicious act by a drunken fan.  Apparently, he heaved a brick through a plate

      glass window.   Spontaneously , the crowd got ugly.  A melee was soon in progress."

      "The police have taken the perpetrator into custody and the somewhat degenerate mob has been  

        dispersed."

      "That's all we have time for, I'm afraid.  Thank you, John."

      "Thank you, Kevin.  John Spectacle, All News Radio, Pike's Market, Downtown Seattle."

     Synonyms

     Melee:  fracas, brawl, noisy quarrel

     Malicious:  hateful, spiteful, beastly, bitter, resentful

     Degenerate:  deteriorated to a lower moral state, degraded, corrupt

     Custody:  care and responsibility

      Incipient:  inchoate, just starting to happen or to be, undeveloped

Course Requirements

No requirements are necessary to partake of this course other than a love for horses.

Course Goals

This course aims to provide you with solutions that work for some of the most common issues faced by horse owners.  These include:

  • How to tell if your horse has colic and
  • What to do about it.
  • How to check your horse's vital signs and report them to your vet
  • How to administer medicine with a minimum of fuss
  • How to deworm with a minimum of fuss
  • How to load your horse when he refuses
  • How to teach your horse to be bathed
  • How to teach your horse to accept a farrier
  • And much more.

1.1   There is quite a number of "Friendly Allies"

"Friendly allies" or "Perfect Cognates" are words spelled the same way and have the same meaning in German and English


 
1.2   The only difference here:   

The pronounciation is slightly different 

Nouns are always capitalized in German

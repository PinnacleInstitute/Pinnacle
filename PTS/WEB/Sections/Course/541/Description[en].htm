Technical Requirements

Netscape or Internet Explorer (versions 4.0 or higher).   Browsers should be capable of accepting cookies; SVGA Hi-color mode 800x600 resolution display.  Macromedia Shockwave 6.02 or higher.

Course Requirements

Familiarity with the basic concepts of PC hardware.

Course Goals

Identify the components of a PC Identify the types of motherboards Identify the components of a motherboard Identify the system resources used in a PC Describe the process of assembling a PC

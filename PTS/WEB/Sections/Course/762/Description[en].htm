Course Goals

  • Cope with stressful change
  • Understand loss, grieving and growing
  • Resolve guilt and anger
  • Make peace with the past
  • Tap into your wisdom and spiritual strength
  • Be happy right now-even if your life isn't perfect
  • Take advantage of the choices created by change
  • Embrace reality, yet maintain a tough-minded optimism
  • Find a deeper level of emotional healing, personal fulfillment and self-understanding

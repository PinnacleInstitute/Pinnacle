Admission Requirements

All applicants must complete a registration application (must be signed by a parent or guardian if the applicant is under 18 years of age). All US applicants must have a high school diploma or its equivalent (GED); International students must have their country's equivalent. Students must agree to maintain the NGH standard codes of conduct and strict code of ethics.

Course Description

Level I - Basic Hypnotherapy Training

Upon completion, you'll receive certification of having successfully completed the NGH Level I curriculum in Hypnotherapy Training at New York Awareness Center.

The Level-I course includes:

  • Introduction - Goals and Training

  • Suggestibility tests

  • The classification of subjects

  • Three-step self hypnosis

  • How to hypnotize, conduct depth tests and awakening procedures

  • Applications of hypnosis

  • Complete programs for:

    • Smoking Cessation

    • Weight Management 

    • Stress Management  

    • Other scripts and tips for your hypnotherapy practice

  • Practice in class, lots of it!

  • Final exam

Level II - NGH Hypnotherapy Certification

The prerequisite – Completed Level I training. Upon completion of the Level II - Advanced, you will qualify for Certification through the National Guild of Hypnotists (NGH), the most prestigious national organization in this industry.

The Level-II course includes:

  • Hypnosis, the technical definition

  • First session with a client

  • Behavioral assessment - goal setting - reinforcement

  • Guided Imagery Psychology in Hypnosis

  • Sessions two, three and four with a client

  • Designing and teaching a six hour seminar

  • Age and Past life regressions

  • Advanced hypnosis techniques

  • Anxiety, Pain Control, Performance enhancement techniques

  • Self hypnosis techniques

  • Marketing your business strategies and e-Marketing

  • Legal and ethical considerations

  • Final exam

Additional practice and credit hours are achieved by preparation of the homework in any of the following:

  • preparation of a hypnosis related talk, article, seminar, or a Ericksonian therapeutic tale

  • preparation of a 6-hour hypnosis seminar

  • independent study project

  • watching and discussing hypnosis videos from NGH library

  • supervised practice

This training program offers a comprehensive and in depth approach to the spectrum of hypnotherapy techniques. It is designed to offer those preparing for a professional hypnotherapy practice the greatest advantage. Through the NGH curriculum, proven therapeutic programs for helping individuals resolve problem areas in their lives your clients benefit therapeutically; (mind, body, emotions and spirit). Additionally, record keeping, effective marketing, web-marketing and setting your web-site, advertising, and many other aspects of "how to" set up your successful practice are taught.

The course includes 100 required hours of instruction, homework and practice. Classroom: 60 hours and 40 hours of homework, practice sessions. A dynamic class format consisting of lectures, Q & A, demonstrations of therapeutic processes and techniques, hands-on supervised practice using the latest tools and techniques learned in class, in addition practical self-healing techniques for stress, and tools for well-being to help yourself and others is covered in class. Through direct experience, students receive the most valuable aspects of each approach in hypnotherapy. This has proven to be very successful for accelerated learning, and the students enjoy the process. Homework will include learning to write scripts, Ericksonian style metaphors, self-suggestions and other types of practice.

Graduates can utilize hypnotherapy as a career and become self-employed or find employment with medical/health professionals, or alternative health care professionals. Upon successful completion of this course, you will be awarded your NGH Hypnotherapy Certification.

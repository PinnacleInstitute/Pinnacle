Prerequisites: None

Performance Objectives:

 Upon completion of this course, the student will be able to:

  1. Recognize, pronounce, spell, define and understand medical terminology related to the structure and function of cells and tissues.
  2. Recognize, pronounce, spell, define and understand medical terminology related to the structure and function of organs, systems and body organization.
  3. Identify and describe the structure of cells, tissues, organs, and systems.
  4. Identify and use appropriate medical references and other resources.

Course Materials

Essentials of Anatomy and Physiology, 7th Ed., Elaine N. Marieb; Anatomy & Physiology Coloring Book, 7th Ed., Elaine N. Marieb

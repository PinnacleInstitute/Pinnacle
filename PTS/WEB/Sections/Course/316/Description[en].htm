Technical Requirements

Must be in good health and physically able to meet the course goals.

Course Requirements

Able to student and comprehend physical and written materials presented.

Course Goals

Learn to tie Obi, Fold Dogi, Dojo Rules, Instructor Expectations, Student Creed, Conduct Rules, Kiba dachi, Masuba dachi, Jun Tsuki, Mae Geri, Jodan Age Uke, Chudan Soto/Uchi Uke, Hikite Uchi, Gedan Barai, Heisoku dachi, Zenkutsu dachi, Tai Sabaki Zenkutsu dachi, Jodan Soto Uke, Chudan Soto Uke, Chudan Harai Uke, Ushiro Geri, Seiken oizuki, Gyakuzuki, Haito Uchi, Yoko Geri, Jodan Uchi Uke, Morote Uke, Shiko dachi, Uraken Uchi, Keta jun-tsuki/Zenkutsu dachi, Mawashi Geri, Kokutsu dachi, Shuto Uchi, Shuto Uke.  Kata: Taikyoku Shodan, Ten-no-kata

Course Materials

Introduction to Karate manual, VHS machine, and uniform essential for training.

Grading Policy

Pass or Fail

Sales Management System

Adding New Prospects

Unqualified Prospects
Prospects may be added to your Sales Management System in two different ways.  If the prospect has not specified an interest and still needs to be qualified, you will want to email the new prospect a specific lead program to see if they are interested. 

1. Select the "Leads - Email New Prospects" menu option at the top of the page. 

2. Select the desired lead program from the list. 

3. Optionally, click the "Preview" button to view the selected lead program.

4. Enter up to ten prospect names and email addresses.

5. Click the "Add" button.

The system will automatically email an invitation to each new prospect with a link to the selected lead program.  Each of your new prospects will also be entered in your Sales Management System.  You will receive an email immediately after the new prospect views the lead program and another one when they reply to the lead program by clicking the "Submit" button.
 

Qualified Prospects
Prospects may be added directly into your Sales Management System manually.  A qualified prospect has already expressed interest and is ready to be converted into a customer.  Their is no need to send a lead program to the qualified prospect because the lead program is designed to get their interest, which you already have done.

1. Select the "Prospect - Add New Prospect" menu option at the top of the page.

2. Select the desired Sales Campaign

3. Optionally, click the "Description" button to view the selected Sales Campaign.

4. Enter the Prospect's name

5. Enter any other optional prospect information.

6. Click the "Add" button.

The new prospect will be added in to your Sales Management System.  The system will not send anything to your new qualified prospect.  It is now your responsibility to convert your qualified prospect in to a  customer.

 

Searching for Existing Prospects

You can search for your existing prospects on the Sales Management Page by selecting a prospect field to search by (e.g. name, contact, prospect #, email or entry date, lead program #, sales campaign #, description).  Enter text to search for.  You can enter a partial phrase like "smit", will find every prospect name starting with "smit" (e.g. smith, smithsonian).  The prospect name and description will search for any word contained anywhere within the field. 

Click the "GO" button to list the selected prospects. Leave the search field blank to list all prospects - twenty at a time, sorted by the search field.

Click the name of the prospect to go to the prospect detail page for editing.

Sales Management System

Using your Lead Programs

Overview
The lead system empowers you to generate your own highly qualified leads with very targeted lead programs.  The following instructions will lead you through the steps to successfully use your lead system. 

1. Select the "Leads - Lead Programs" menu option at the top of the page. 

2. View the available lead programs by clicking [Preview]. 

3. Chose a program to use and click [Usage] to obtain the associated web address (URL) and promotion ideas.

After clicking [Usage], next to the lead program of your choice, your lead program personal web address will be displayed.  This web address, or Link, will take your prospects to the Lead Program you selected.


How to use your lead program web address 

First - Copy the Link                       

Second - Paste your Link into an email, message board, forum, etc. 

Second (Cont.) Create a custom link You can use Microsoft Word to create a Text Link. 

Example:
100% Home Based Business

By creating a Text Link, you will make your personal lead program web address (URL) easier to read and more user friendly
Step One - Type and highlight your text Step Two - Right Click with your mouse while pointing at the text, then click Hyperlink or on the menu select - Insert, then Hyperlink

Step Three - Type or paste the your link in the field indicated

Step Four - Copy the linked text into an email etc. (see Step 2 above)

Third - If you chose to advertise through newspapers, internet search engines, or ezines (online magazines or newsletters), you may want to consider creating you own domain name.  This is a simple web address that is easy to remember (www.domain-name.com).  You can set up your own domain name to forward directly to your lead program.  For more information on setting up and using your own domain name, click here: Domain Registration Tutorial

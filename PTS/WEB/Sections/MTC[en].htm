Ask your Salesperson for a Demonstration

Pinnacle provides companies with their own custom, private-labeled Multimedia Training and Communication Platform.

Companies can easily build custom multimedia courseware for their employees or independent agents on product knowledge, policies and procedures, safety courses, sales skills, etc. 

Companies can regularly publish multimedia communication programs including new product rollouts, conventions, company announcements, management messages, leadership interviews, employee recognition, etc. 

Companies can define their own lists of recommended courses and they can view management reports to monitor the training and certification progress of their employees.

Companies receive all the standard Pinnacle benefits including personal assessments, testing, professional certificates, and the complete catalog of Pinnacle courses.

Companies can also qualify for volume discounts on the membership fees.

 

MTC Features

1. Custom Website:  The Company will receive customizable web pages that are branded specifically for them, including a public home page, a member home page and a FAQ page for their signed in members.  The Company can also access the Pinnacle online page editor for customizing their pages and promoting their courses and programs.

2. Custom Header:  The Company will receive a customized header that will appear at the top of all web pages. The header will include the Company logo and a small “powered by Pinnacle” logo.

3. Custom Training Courses:  The Company can build multimedia courseware, which may support product knowledge, policies and procedures, safety courses, sales skills, motivational courses, etc.

4. Custom Communication Services:  The Company can regularly publish multimedia communication programs including new product rollouts, conventions, company announcements, management messages, leadership interviews, recognition, etc.

5. Custom Communication Forums:  The Company members can interact through Company and course related online message boards and chat rooms.

6. Custom Testing:  The Company can define custom tests to certify and recognize its members.

7. Custom Certificates:  The MTC includes course certificates with the Company’s logo.

8. Custom Course Lists:  The Company can define multiple lists of recommended public and private courses for its different employee departments.

9. Personal Assessments:  Members receive assessments to better understand their strengths and weaknesses and they receive a roadmap to focus on their most important areas of improvement.

10. Pinnacle Course Catalog:  The Company members and their families can access the complete Pinnacle course catalog to improve themselves in all aspects of their lives.

11. Company Reports:  The Company can review reports on the training status of its members.

12. Quick Setup:  The Company’s MTC can be deployed within 72 hours.

13. International Support:  The MTC has been architected to support multiple languages as needed.

14. Minimal Membership:  The MTC can be implemented for as few as 25 members.

15. Discount Program:  The MTC includes an optional discount program to reduce the cost of membership.

16. Free Company Hosting:  The MTC and all custom training courses and communications programs are hosted at no cost to the Company.  The only charge is for the individual memberships.

17. Volume Discounts:  The Company can qualify for volume discounts from 15% to 50% off of the regular $50 per month per member fees.

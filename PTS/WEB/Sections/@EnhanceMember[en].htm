Pinnacle Member Enhancements!

(updated 9/13/06 11:00am CST)

9/13/05 Lead Program Company Name: The member's company name is displayed on the lead programs if they are a company.
9/7/05 Goal Updates: A Goal status can now be updated from the detail page or from the sub goal list by clicking the [start], [complete], or [clear] links following the goal name. You can go directly to the Goal edit page by clicking the [edit] link following the goal name in the sub goal list.
9/5/05 Goal Reminders: A Member can request to be notified by email that a projected completion date for a goal is coming up.
8/16/05 Mentor Notification Checkboxes: Mentor can now select check boxes for notifications.
8/1/05 Lead Redistribution: Any member can redistribute their leads to any other member. A lead (prospect) may be designated as non-distributeable. The Sales Management System - "Leads" menu option includes "Distribute Leads" where the member can enter a member # to distribute the selected leads to, and a lead program to list leads from which to distribute.  The giving and receiving members will both receive a confirmation email with a detailed list of the distributed leads.
7/19/06 Mentor Notification: A mentor can now request to be notified when a mentoree has completed a class, assessment, certification, goal, project or sales.
7/12/06 My Classes - Course #: The course # is displayed after the course name in the member's My Classes list.
7/1/06 Lead Program Viewing Statistics: The system now tracks the lead program viewing statisics for a prospect that is emailed a lead program.  The prospect information includes the number of times the lead program was viewed, the list of page numbers that were viewed, and the number of time the prospect replies to the lead program. 
6/26/06 Email Prospect Templates: Prospect Mail Templates can be defined for each sales campaign. The templates can contain replaceable tags for the prospect and member information.  A list of available templates for the specific sales campaign is available when the member sends an email to the prospect. 
6/23/06 Search Active Prospects: The Sales Management System can now search within all active prospects. Select "Active" from the status list.
6/11/06 Member Email Prospect: The member can now view a list of emails sent to a prospect by selecting the "Mail" tab on the Prospect page.
6/11/06 Member Email Signature: The member can now specify an email signature to be used for emails sent to their prospects.  Select the "Contact" tab on the My Info page.
6/8/06 Email Prospect: The member can now email a prospect directly from the prospect page.  The email is logged in the prospect notes.
5/30/06 Sales Lifecycle: The member can now view a sales lifecycle of all the system features organized in a chronological time-line.
4/28/06 Attachment Status: Attachments have a new status of "private" that will not appear in the search engine.
4/28/06 Affiliate Back-Office: Affiliates can now access the Affiliate Back Office to view thier Lead Program traffic statistics and lead program usage instructions.
4/28/06 Lead Program Traffic: Traffic Reports can be viewed by member or affiliate.
4/28/06 Lead Program Access: Lead Programs can be optionally accessed by members and/or affiliates.
4/28/06 Affiliate Tabs: Affiliate information is organized with folder tabs.
4/28/06 Affiliate Terms: Members can specify the terms of the individual affiliate partnership.
4/27/06 Member Afiliates: Members can manage their own affiliate partners from the sales management system. 
4/27/06 Member Payments: Members can view their payments from the support menu. 
4/27/06 Prospect Documents: Documents can be attached to a prospect. 
4/27/06 Project Name on Phases and Tasks: The Project name is displayed at the top of the page for phases and tasks. 
4/27/06 Document Search Engine: Help text added - Documents can be listed by the search engine only if their status is active, they haven't expired, and their security level is accessible by the member. 
4/7/06  Project What's New Report: The report for multiple projects now lists all projects that have been worked on anywhere in the project/phase/tasks.  The report for a single project lists all phases and tasks that have been worked on. 
4/7/06  Project/Phase Change Date: The last date the status of the project, phases, or tasks was changed is display in the upper right hand corner of the project/phase.
3/25/06  Optimized sales prospect page: Added section to the page to easily view to information. 3/9/06  Lead Management System: All users can view thier company's marketing strategy and they can promote self-replicated lead programs to generate leads that are feed directly into the sales management system. 
1/15/06  Project Collaboration System: All users can manage their projects with groups of other people. They can define projects with multiple phases, tasks, notes, documents and a discussion board and/or conference room. 
12/16/05  Information Shortcuts: All users can create personal shortcuts to any important information like classes, assessments, goals, documents, etc. 
10/16/05  Member Goal Assignment: Goals now indicate at the top of the detail page, who assigned the goal.  It may be assigned by the member himself, one of his mentors, his business membership, or his company. New goals can be added by others from the Member Goal Report.
10/3/05  Document Search By Date: Members can search for documents that are new or have been updated since a specified date.
9/29/05  Course Series: Members can search for courses that beolong to  series of related courses. If a course belongs to a series it will display the series number after its own course number. Clicking on the series number will display all courses in the series.
9/9/05  Mentoring Program: Members can specify another member as their Mentor to assist with meeting their objectives.
8/26/05  Advanced Search Help: Search pages for courses, documents and questions includes a link after the "Search" button  to a popup help page on advanced search techniques.
8/25/05  Document & Question Dates: Searches for Documents or Questions will display the date of the requested item.
8/25/05  Search Engine Similar Keywords: The Search Engines for Courses, Documents and Questions now search for keywords that are similar to the specified keyword. for example "step" will return "Steps" and "Stepping".
8/8/05  Member Support System: The Member can optionally access a support search engine to find information in Q&As or Documents.
6/14/05  Goal Date Changes: If a goal commit date or complete date is changed, the change is logged in the notes and is not editable by the member (editable only by managers).
6/14/05  Sub Goals: Sub Goals are listed on the same page as the parent goal detail page.
6/14/05  Copy Goals: Members can copy an existing goal. Copy includes all sub goals and reminder notes.
6/12/05  Training Buddy Goals: Training Buddy displays the Member’s goal statistics.
6/5/05  My Goals: Member’s can set and track company, department and individual goals.
6/5/05  Certification Memberships: A new member status is available for certification only purposes.  They can only access certification tests, user profile, electronic suggestion boxes and surveys.   
6/5/05  Validate Credit Cad Numbers: Credit card numbers are validated for valid type, length, and number.
6/5/05  Billing Electronic Checks: only available for USA payment addresses.
6/5/05  New Member Enrollment Form: The new member enrollment form is separated into three separate pages; 1) Membership program selection, 2) Member contact info, and 3) optional billing info.
4/22/05  Performance Prescriptions: Assessments can provide a personal prescription to a member based on their answers.
4/22/05 Diagnostic Assessments: Assessments can ask a series of probing questions to troubleshoot a situation and identify an answer to a specific need or problem.
4/15/05  Take Course Notes: Members can take notes during a class and print the notes if needed. 
4/9/05  Expectations: Added Training Buddy Expectation statistics and review
4/3/05  Training Buddy: Created Training Buddy for members
3/25/05  Suggestion Response: Member can review the response from management to their suggestions.

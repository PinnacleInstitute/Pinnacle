Allard is the Director of Change & Challenge® and Director of Training at CoachVille Spain.  Every day,  he helps organisations and people of all walks of life clear out whatever is limiting their progress and make a strong connection to the power that is already within them.

Previous to his coaching and training career, Allard shows over 15 years of international experience working in consultancy with global responsibility . His background covers a wide range of industries including clients such as HP, Interbrew, DaimlerChrysler, Dainippon Screen, Ford, Deutsche Bank, Mastercard, Hennis & Mauritz, Kodak, Pirelli Tires, Pioneer, Holiday Inn, Eurostar Chunnel Link, Lotus Software, Lufthansa, Aer Lingus, Lauda Air, Bertolli, DelMonte, Corona Beer, Domino’s Pizza and more.

Allard is a licensed facilitator of Corporate Coach U’s Coaching Clinic ®, a high quality coaching programme aimed at management development. He has trained with the Graduate School of Coaching as well as the School of Small Business Coaching and has studied in person with leaders in the field such as Thomas Leonard, Laura Berman Fortgang, Richard Reardon, Steve Straus or Anthony Robbins.

Allard is a frequent lecturer on the subjects of change, teams, coaching and leadership at MBA programmes throughout Spain. He has lived in Europe and the United States and is fluent in English, Dutch, French and Spanish. He received a Masters Degree in Communications Studies from the University of California and a Bachelors in International Business Administration  from the American University in Paris.

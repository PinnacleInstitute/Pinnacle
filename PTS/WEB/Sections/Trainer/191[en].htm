Clay Neves is a Personal Marketing Coach and Sales Engineer with 23 years of experience in sales, marketing and sales management.  He has spent over 10 years in the training and development industry including sales, leadership, communications, and computer technology training.   He specializes in what he has termed Integrated Personal Marketing entailing all facets of positioning, packaging and promoting his clients, including relationship marketing, telemarketing, strategic sales process design, interactive dialogue scripting, customer contact management systems, e-mail broadcasting and web-site development.

He has designed and implemented successful relationship marketing programs for Fortune 500 companies such as Citibank, Pacific Bell, QVC, and Showtime, as well as small businesses and individuals like you.   He serves as Executive Director of the Murray Area Chamber of Commerce in Murray, Utah.  He is a guest instructor for SCORE and the Utah Small Business Development Center.

He is Lieutenant Governor of Marketing for Utah and Idaho for Toastmasters International and is a board member of the Utah Chapter of the National Speakers Association.

Award-winning speaker, coach and trainer, he has guided thousands to greater personal marketing success.

Michel Gagne is an author, speaker, facilitator and International Executive and Corporate Coach with more than 30 years of experience.  Excellent motivator, Michel has worked with several Olympic Athletes from Canada and abroad. Michel speaks French, English and Bahasa Malaysia and has been in Asia for more than fourteen years.

 

Michel Gagne is a Certified Coach Facilitator with Corporate Coach U. International and a Certified Counselor with the International Society of Mental Training and Excellence. He is also a Certified and Licensed Leader with the Legacy Leadership Institute of Dallas, Texas and a Certified NLP Coach with the Lambent do Brazil of Sao Paulo, Brazil.

 

He is a highly skilled communicator and motivator experienced in dealing with the Corporate Sector, Government, Education, and NGO's. He also holds a degree from the University of Montreal, Canada.

 

Michel has directed courses and lectured in all of Canada; in the European Countries of United Kingdom, France, Switzerland, Sweden and Russia; in the Caribbean Islands of Puerto Rico and Dominica; in the West African Countries of Senegal, Guinea and Mauritania; in Cairo for the Gulf Region; in the Asian Countries of India, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Philippines, Indonesia, Malaysia, Brunei, Cambodia, Singapore, Taiwan and China.

 

Currently based in Malaysia, he was previously attached to a Government Agency in Malaysia, as Manager of High Performance Programs. He is involved in Management and High Performance for many years at various levels; initially, at the City of Montreal in Staff Training and Stress Management programs; the Coaching Association of Canada in High Performance Programs; the Sri Lanka Government in Youth at Risks and many more.

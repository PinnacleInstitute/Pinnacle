Experience

Elizabeth has been passionately involved with horses since the first moment that she could.  Today Elizabeth trains horses at her ranch in Montana, where she resides with her husband Michael, three children and too many critters to mention.  Elizabeth uses nothing but natural horsemanship.  One of her fondest moments was meeting and working with John Lyons.  Elizabeth states that she "has never seen a man more in tune with horses."

 

Publications

Elizabeth has authored three horse related courses, Basic Horse Care, Horse Training for Respect and Trust and How to Buy a Horse with confidence.  Elizabeth also has a short story in the works with Chicken Soup for the Soul.

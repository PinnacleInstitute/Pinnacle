Og Mansino fans, who number in the millions, flock to hear him speak whenever he fulfills one of his rare speaking engagements.  And they are not disappointed.  There is none of the usual shouting of inspirational cliches, timeworn platitudes, or dull preaching that they are so accustomed to hearing and a hush usually falls over the audience as this living legend shares his most personal thoughts and experiences in a calm, almost conversational voice, producing a warm one-on-one relationship with everyone present.  A respected friend has come to advise them... and they are ready to listen.

Education

Nebraska Wesleyan University and Southern Methodist University. Taught Public Relations Management for six years at the University of Dallas Graduate School of Management.  Accreditation status and election to the College of Fellows, Public Relations Society of America.

Experience

Alice M. Dykeman, APR, Fellow-PRSA, started her career as a journalist and then discovered public relations offered more diversity to use her writing skills and other talents.  The career path included an international entertainment company, a public relations and film company, Methodist Medical Center as public relations director, U.S. Small Business Administration as regional public relations officers and establishment of Dykeman Associates Inc., helping organizations achieve their marketing and public relations goals since l974.

Awards

Numerous awards in her 11-year health career.  As president of Dykeman Associates she was presented the Matrix award by Women in Communications twice, external communication award by District 5 of International Association of Business Communicators for directing a campaign to help save the Battleship Texas through aluminum can recycling, lifetime communicator award by the Dallas-Fort Worth Chapter of  Religion Communicators Council and the Norm Teich award for dedication to public service by the Dallas Chapter of the Public Relations Society of America.

Publications

Many articles in national and regional publications in healthcare, public relations, marketing, visual communications, crisis communications planning, small business economy, entrepreneurship and related fields.

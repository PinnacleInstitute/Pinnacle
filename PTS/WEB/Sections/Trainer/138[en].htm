Jodee Redmond is a former independent Internet Researcher with StudyWeb. This position involved locating and reviewing Web sites with educational content on a variety of topics. One such topic was Internet Safety. Jodee had the opportunity to view hundreds of sites related to this topic. As an avid Internet user and a parent, the subject of Internet Safety is one that Jodee is very passionate about. Like many things in life, the Internet can be a wonderful resource, but users need to have knowledge about some basic safety rules and good judgment to get the most out of their online experience.

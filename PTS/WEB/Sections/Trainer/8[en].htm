In 1994 WinTech, Inc. spun-off from Microsoft to become a Certified Microsoft Solution Provider working within the business community to develop Internet Business Solutions for companies leveraging the power of the Internet in meeting their business objectives. 

 

WinTech specializes in the development of client-server multi-lingual Internet Business Solutions and is capable of supporting over 125 international languages and dialects, including the translation services for 29 major languages.  WinTech has an impressive track record of successful Internet solutions including a powerful toolbox of specialized solutions.

Philosopher, genius, motivational counselor, and business executive. Those are just some of the hats worn by the greatest motivational speaker of all time- -Jim Rohn.  His seminars cover a span of over 39 years.  He has addressed more than 6,000 audiences and 4 million people worldwide.  He has helped to motivate and train an entire generation of personal development trainers including Anthony Robbins, Les Brown, Harvey MacKay and Danielle Kennedy.

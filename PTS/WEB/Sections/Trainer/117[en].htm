Experience

    Shirley Knapp is an internationally recognized motivational trainer and author.  Her focus is consulting individual and groups on personal growth into productivity using intuition and self-empowerment techniques.  For over 20 years she has been in the marketing field beginning with the ownership of an advertising agency, and moving on to incorporate these techniques into her home business.  She has done speaking engagements, online classes, and media coverage, to promote her book SUSTAINING JOY and her CD ROM class, which are focused on walking individuals and groups through the steps of developing their intuition to overcome obstacles that stand in the way of prosperity.

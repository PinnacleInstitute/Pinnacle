David E. Stanley takes you into the gates of the Graceland Mansion where he spent 17 years living, growing up, and eventually working for his world famous stepbrother Elvis Presley.

From Elvis's moments of glory to his untimely death, David shares what it was like growing up as part of Rock and Roll's first family, and how he turned the adversities or his surrealistic upbringing into personal triumph.

David E. Stanley is an internationally renowned motivational speaker, best selling author and creator of Solutionary Dynamics. From the Shadows of the King to Solutionary Dynamics - David empowers audiences, companies and individuals to reach and maintain new levels of excellence in their personal and professional lives.

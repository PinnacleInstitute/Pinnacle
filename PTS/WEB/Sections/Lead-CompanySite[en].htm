So What's The Big Problem With Your Self-Replicated Company Website?


If you're like any other network marketer trying to prospect and recruit online you probably have already figured out that these replicated websites that most Network Marketing companies hand out for free to their distributors don't really convert visitors into signups or leads very well...

And Why Is That?

It's simple, the websites that most of these companies are handing out are providing your visitors with way too much information and no real defined course of action.

When you go and present someone with a whole pile of information right away you are creating confusion.

Confusion leads the visitor towards indecision.

And this will have your visitor clicking the "back" button in their browser and getting outta there. 

You probably already know that when doing any type of prospecting or recruiting on the Internet you need to have fresh people to talk to.  Not only that but you need their contact information as well, so you can follow up with them and start forging a relationship with them.

Most company sites do not supply this.  They do not place a lead capture form on your replicated website.  So it's left up to your visitors to contact you.  And 9 times out of ten we both know this doesn't happen unless they are a real go-getter.

The companies that do provide you with a lead capture form on your site usually collect the leads themselves and then want to sell them back to you!

Having your own lead capture page is a quick solution for this problem.

With a custom lead capture page you are able to present your opportunity in a much softer, simpler way.  When you make it very easy for your prospect to take the next step they most likely will.

With a lead capture page we aren't trying to totally sell the prospect on your opportunity right away and force an hour or two of reading on them...

Think of it as more of a teaser page.  We are simply trying to entice them to request more information.

And because of the simplicity, you can believe it works!

Have a look at your current company issued website and see what you think.

Would it scare you away at first glance?

When it comes to prospecting on the Internet the simpler and more direct your website is the more visitors you will be able to convert into leads.

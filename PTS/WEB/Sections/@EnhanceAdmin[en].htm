Pinnacle Company Administrator Enhancements!

(updated 9/7/06 10:00am CST)

9/7/06 Goal Templates: Goal templates can be created for a company by selecting the "Goal Templates" menu option on the company or folder detail page.
8/8/06 Lead Program SEO Blocking: A Lead Program can be blocked from the search engines by specifying a blank title in the SEO Settings.
6/26/06 Email Prospect Templates: Prospect Mail Templates can be defined for each sales campaign. The templates can contain replaceable tags for the prospect and member information.  A list of available templates for the specific sales campaign is available when the member sends an email to the prospect. 
6/8/06 Email Prospect: The member can now email a prospect directly from the prospect page.  The email is logged in the prospect notes.
5/30/06 Sales Lifecycle: The member welcome page can now define the sales lifecycle of all the system features organized in a chronological time-line.
5/26/06 Perpetual Marketing Newsletter: A newletter on recruiting with Perpetual Marketing is available to be used by any client.
5/16/06 Company Member Options: Member functionality can be enabled/disabled for each individual item.
5/16/06 Prospect Search: Prospects can now be searched for by lead program #, sales campaign #, and any description contents.
5/16/06 NewsLetters: Companies can now publish multiple newsletters for automated subscription and distribution. 
5/6/06 Clear Lead Program Traffic Stats: An employee can clear the traffic statistics for a lead program from the traffic report page.  They can be cleared for all, member or affiliate based on the information passed into the traffic report page. 
5/5/06 Duplicate Member Email Addresses: If a new member attempts to join with an existing email address within the same company, they will not be added, but will be sent to a page telling them that the email address is already used, or that the email address is suspended.  They will also have the option to request the logon name and password be emailed to the email address we have on file.  If the member is being added from the auto-add page (0422) and the email address exists, they will not be added and the logon and password will be automatically sent.
5/4/06 External Prospect Copy: A prospect can be copied to an external system as defined by its sales campaign. 
5/3/06 Discussion Board Message Delete: Company administrators and Program Managers are now given administrator rights on their discussion boards and can delete message threads.  They can also update their user group in the "Edit User Profile" option for the discussion board.
4/28/06 Attachment Status: Attachments have a new status of "private" that will not appear in the search engine.
4/28/06 Affiliate Back-Office: Affiliates can now access the Affiliate Back Office to view thier Lead Program traffic statistics and lead program usage instructions.
4/28/06 Lead Program Traffic: Traffic Reports can be viewed by company, member or affiliate.
4/28/06 Lead Program Access: Lead Programs can be optionally accessed by members and/or affiliates.
4/28/06 Affiliate Tabs: Affiliate information is organized with folder tabs.
4/28/06 Affiliate Terms: Members can specify the terms of the individual affiliate partnership.
4/27/06 Member Afiliates: Members can manage their own affiliate partners from the sales management system. 
4/27/06 Member Payments: Members can view their payments from the support menu. 
4/27/06 Prospect Documents: Documents can be attached to a prospect. 
4/27/06 Project Name on Phases and Tasks: The Project name is displayed at the top of the page for phases and tasks. 
4/27/06 Document Search Engine: Help text added - Documents can be listed by the search engine only if their status is active, they haven't expired, and their security level is accessible by the member. 
4/12/06 2-Tier Affiliate Program: An Affiliate can refer other affiliates and be compensated according to the company's terms.
4/12/06 Lead Program Affiliate Tracking: The Affiliate is tracked along with the traffic stats on a lead program.
4/6/06  Affiliate Management System: The first version is available for companies to manage their affiliate partners. Prospects now have an AffiliateID field that is set from the lead program parameter.
4/5/06  Lead Program Affiliate Parameter: The lead program page now supports a parameter (a) for the AffiliateID. e.g.  www.pinnaclep.com/lp.asp?p=5&m=84&a=1
4/5/06  Lead Program Distribution List: A Lead Program can be setup to distribute its leads to a group of members one at a time in a round robin fashion. if a lead program is accessed without a member then the next member in the distribution list is displayed.
3/25/06  Optimized sales prospect page: Added section to the page to easily view to information. 3/23/06  Auto Register Courses: A member can be auto registered for a course and taken directly to the class to view with the following URL. http://www.pinnaclep.com/1330.asp?courseid=x&register=1
3/9/06  Lead Management System: A company may design its own marketing strategies and lead programs. 
12/29/05  Sales Prospecting System: A company may design its own custom sales system for managing prospects. 
12/16/05  Information Shortcuts: All users can create personal shortcuts to any important information like classes, assessments, goals, documents, etc. 
11/8/05  Document Search Security Level: When a member searches for a document or views documents attached to a lesson, if the document has a security level greater than zero it is displayed in red. 
10/24/05  Company Certification Report by Group: Companies can view certification reports summarized by Group and Role or by Business and Role. 
10/22/05  Custom Member Pages and Training Buddies: A custom member page and/or training buddy image can be specified based on the member's group and/or security level.  The default member page, member.htm, is appended with the Member's group  and/or security level.  For example, if the member's group is Sales and his security level is 2, the system will attempt to load the custom page with the filename of MemberSales2.htm.  If the file doesn't exist it will look for an alternate file in the following order; MemberSales1.htm, MemberSales.htm, Member2.htm, Member1.htm, and finally the default file of Member.htm. The same is also done for the default training buddy image of TBImage.gif and the Training Buddy page name specified in the Company options. New Member and Training Buddy pages can be created with the Company's Pages, with the desired filenames.  New Training Buddy images can be uploaded with the Image Manager accessed from the Company's Pages. The image filename should match the desired naming convention (e.g. TBImageSales1.gif).
10/21/05  Company Development Membership Fee: The Development Membership Fee for each company can be specified on the Company options page.
10/21/05  Folder Course Report: The folder report listing a status summary of all classes taken in the folder now includes a link on the course name to view all student details for each class taken.
10/5/05  Documents & Questions Search Indexing: New or updated Documents and Questions are reindexed for the support search engine every day every 15 minutes from 6:00am to 6:00pm CST and every night at midnight.
8/23/05  Member Security Levels: Each Company can define their own security Levels for its Members. The Security Level is applied to Document attachments to lessons, to Questions, and Question topics. Each Member can access all resources that have thier Security Level or lower.
8/19/05  Hide Lesson Content: If a lesson has no Content displayed then the Content editor is hidden. 
8/15/05  Class Lesson Documents Only: If a lesson has no "media type" and has no "content layout" then the top row with "Take Notes" and the Timer are not displayed.  the "Mark Complete" checkbox is also not displayed. 
8/15/05  Lesson Attached Document Links: An attached file to a lesson can now be a URL link to a document anywhere on the Internet. 
8/8/05  Member Support System: The Program Manager can optionally specify member support with Q&As and/or Documents on the Company detail page. 
8/5/05 Document Manager:  All lesson attachments can be listed for a trainer, company or course from the corresponding detail page.
8/2/05 Company Subnet: Each Company can specify a subnet on the Company profile page for accessing their Pinnacle training website.
6/16/05 Member Search by Business/Group/Role: Can search for members by business membership #, Group or Role.
6/16/05 Company Email Lists: can build company mail lists for the Email Manager based on 13 different queries with optional parameters.
6/15/05 Email Manager: Send scheduled messages to select members.
6/5/05 Member Group and Role: Enter the Group a member is in (e.g. Store 123, Operations) and the role they play (Manager, Programmer).  This is used for the Certification and Accountability Reports.
6/5/05 Accountability System: Member’s can commit to company, department and individual goals with specific deadlines.  Management can track the progress and status of the member’s goals to facilitate the development of an execution culture as well as for employee reviews.
6/5/05 Certification Reporting: can view report on detailed certifications for a specific member or a summary report for a business membership or the entire company. 
6/5/05 Master Certifications: A certification may require the passing of multiple other certifications.
6/5/05 Certification Asssessments: Members cannot edit their completed assessments.  If they have a passing grade they can print a certification certificate. 
6/5/05 Survey Question Status: Can now be set to active/inactive.
6/5/05 Assessment Question Status: Can now be set to active/inactive.
6/5/05 Membership Status Update: A member's status may be changed from a separate form.  If the membership is paid up, it will be changed after the paid-up date.  If it is being changed to active, it can be scheduled to be changed back to something else after a specified number of months.
6/5/05 Business Membership Max Accounts: A business membership may specify a maximum number of allowable full paid membership accounts. 
6/5/05 Business Memberships: A new business membership is setup as a "Bill Member", with pricing copied from the company record.
6/5/05 Billing Electronic Checks: only available for USA payment addresses.
6/5/05 New Member Enrollment Form: The new member enrollment form is separated into three separate pages; 1) Membership program selection, 2) Member contact info, and 3) optional billing info.
5/4/05 Company Available Assessments: Assessments link for a company displays a list of all active assessments available to its members, both public and company.
5/4/05 Member Assessments Report: View a list of all non-private Member Assessments for the selected assessment from the company available assessment list.
4/22/05 Performance Prescriptions: Assessments can provide a personal prescription to a member based on their answers.
4/22/05 Diagnostic Assessments: Assessments can ask a series of probing questions to troubleshoot a situation and identify an answer to a specific need or problem.
4/11/05 New Member Email: Option for system to send email notification to newly added members.  defaults to No, system sets to Yes when company status changes from setup to active. system sets to No when company status changes from active to setup.  accessible from the Company Defaults page.
4/9/05  Expectation Statistics: Added Managing Buddy Expectation statistics
4/9/05  Expectation: Added Expectation field for company folders
4/1/05  Public Folder Members: Updated Public Folders to not Included Members
4/3/05  Suggestion Status Filter: Added ability to search for Suggestions with a Status filter
4/3/05  Managing Buddy: Created Managing Buddy for administrators
4/3/05  Training Buddy Image: Added ability for each company to upload their own custom Training Buddy Image and a custom Training Buddy information page.
3/25/05  Suggestion Response: Added response field to suggestions, tracking the response date and person

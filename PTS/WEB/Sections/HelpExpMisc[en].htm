Any other expense for running your home based business may be eligible for a tax deduction including the following:

 

Advertising and Marketing Expenses

Bank Fees

Interest Fees

Legal and Professional Fees

Meeting / Seminar Fees

Office Expenses

Postage

Rent or Lease of business equipment

Repairs & maintenance of business equipment

Supplies

Taxes and Licenses

Never lose a legitimate deduction.  Even if your business is not profitable in the first few years, you may qualify to take a business loss.  These losses offset your earned income, and whenever possible can significantly reduce taxed owed.  If there is no earned income, you can carry forward these losses to future years, or you can carry back up to three years (when a casualty or theft loss).

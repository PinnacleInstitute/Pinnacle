Everything in sales is based on the marketing strategy.  The following key questions lay the foundation for your sales programs.  You can answer these questions and make them available for all to review by selecting the "Marketing Strategy" option from the "Setup" menu.

  1. What exactly is our product and what does it do?
  2. What is the target market and what is its personality?
  3. What benefit is the target market looking to get out of this product?
  4. What is the Unique Selling Proposition – What makes the product different, better, or more desirable than other similar products available?
  5. How will this be conveyed to the customer?
  6. How will we prove it?
  7. How will it stand out above its competitors?
  8. Who are the top competitors and what are they doing right and what are they doing wrong?

 

Why Lead Programs?

Sadly, too many companies send the click-throughs from promotions to their site's home page. Unless you are offering a single product with a microsite, this can be a big mistake. The average home page doesn't provide a clear path to the product you are advertising, nor is it suited to follow a particular ad, cinch the deal, and bring in the sale. Instead, the average home page provides a half-dozen rabbit trails that your potential customer can explore before she gives up in disgust and impatience. A lead program, on the other hand, is designed to accomplish a specific targeted objective.

 

Lead Management Process

The Lead Management System supports the following process for managing a lead program:

  1. Define Objective
  2. Build Lead Program
  3. Promote Lead Program
  4. Monitor Lead Program
  5. Distribute Leads
  6. Monitor Leads

 

Define Objective

The creation of a lead program starts with the identification of its objective in coordination with the marketing strategy.  A lead program may be focused on various results like; attracting a niche retail customer, attracting sales representatives, or promoting a special program.

 

Build Lead Program  

You can build an unlimited number of lead programs for private use by the company only or for public use by the members.

Design
A lead program should be constructed to support the following design principles:

  1. Lead with your Unique Selling Proposition
  2. Close with your solid Most Wanted Response (MWR)
  3. Enhance Desire with Key Benefits
  4. Create Rationale with Features
  5. Build Trust and Credibility
  6. Eliminate Risk
  7. Make a Compelling Irresistible Offer
  8. Tell Them Exactly What To Do

Construction
The presentation of a lead program is constructed as a series of one or more HTML pages and can include anything possible in a web page.  A new lead program may be created by copying an existing program. Select the "Lead Programs" option from the "Setup" menu.

Lead Program Check List  

  1. Does the Lead Program finish with a solid MWR?
  2. Is the opening line a “Big Gun”?  Does it transmit the major benefit, the USP, to the prospect? Do the next couple of paragraphs build on that?
  3. Scan through each page of the lead program.  Is there a logical progression that builds to the MWR? 
  4. Are all the major benefits covered? Have you enhanced desire by painting word and graphic pictures of the benefits?
  5. Have you helped the prospect create a rationale by describing key distinguishing features?
  6. Does the MWR Closer Page build an offer that makes the MWR irresistible?  Is the offer so good that you’d be afraid to pass it up?
  7. Have you offered proof and major credibility-builders?  Do you get an overall good, believable solid feel from the page?
  8. Have you eliminated the risk (i.e. guarantee, etc.)?  Is that clear to the visitor?
  9. Is it all bundled in a clear call to action where the visitor is told exactly what to do?
  10. Is the visitor reminded of the major benefits again? 
  11. Is there a strong and logical reason why action is required right now? 
  12. Does the visitor understand exactly what she gets?  Don’t take this for granted.
  13. Does the page maintain a “you-oriented” focus throughout the lead program?  Have you eliminated all references to I, we, my, our, and us.
  14. Read it all out loud to a colleague or spouse.  Does anything ring hollow, embarrass you, or just plain doesn’t work? Fix it.
  15. Once it is as good as can be, spell-check it.  Then proof-read for spelling errors missed by the spell checker. Review it for reasonable grammar. Double-check it if a lot of changes are made.

Data Capture
The lead program can capture the visitor's information and save it as a new prospect. Optional fields may be collected as defined by the page.  Extra fields not normally stored on the prospect are saved in the prospect description. You can specify the sales campaign and prospect type for the new prospect. the lead program # is stored with the new prospect. Click the page name from the Lead Program page list, to specify the custom fields to capture.

Personalization
A lead program page may include replaceable tags that identify member specific information like the member’s first name, last name, phone, and email.  Select the "Edit Page Content" link from the Lead Program page list, to include the member's personal information.

Promote Lead Program

Company Promotion
The company may choose to reserve a lead program for promotion by the company only.  The company may promote the lead program using a variety of methods including; advertising, opt-in email blasts, viral marketing, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), etc.

Member Promotion
A member can view a list of all lead programs available for his/her use.  The member’s lead programs can be promoted directly by the member using a variety of methods including, handouts, mailers, networking meetings, business cards, discussion boards, online communities, email, etc.  The URL for the lead program includes a parameter that uniquely identifies the member.  The member’s personal information appears in the lead program.

Dedicated Domain Name
The company or an individual member may purchase an inexpensive dedicate domain name for easier access to a lead program.  Pinnacle can assist with purchasing and setting up the dedicated domain name.

Search Engine Optimization
You may promote your lead programs on the Internet Search Engines.  The construction of the lead program includes support for SEO keywords, description, links, and content. Select the "SEO Settings" button from the Lead Program detail page.  Pinnacle can assist with the purchase of SEO software tools. 

 

Monitor Lead Program  

Traffic Statistics
Pinnacle keeps track of all the traffic visiting each lead program.  Traffic statistics and conversion rates are calculated for each lead program from page to page and to the Most Wanted Response (MWR).  

Search Engine Optimization
Third party tools and some free websites can provide the ability to monitor the ranking of a lead program on the top search engines for a specified keyword search.

 

Distribute Leads

Member Programs
Lead programs promoted by the members are distributed directly to the member as new prospects. The member is automatically notified via email of each new prospect. 

Company Programs
Company generated leads can be distributed to the members manually or automatically based on distribution rules by geography, close rate, availability, etc.  The company may charge a fee for leads, distribute leads for free, or a combination of the two.  Free leads that are not worked in a timely manner may be withdrawn from the member and redistributed to another member.

 

Monitor Leads  

After a qualified lead is captured as a new prospect, it can be tracked to monitor the progress as outlined by the specified sales campaign.
